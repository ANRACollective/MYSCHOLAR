How to Become an Event Manager in Malaysia — MICE, Concerts, and Corporates

Cara Jadi Pengurus Acara di Malaysia — MICE, Konsert, dan Korporat

Event management in Malaysia is a more commercially substantial career than its creative image suggests. Malaysia is a significant MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) destination — Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre (KLCC), Putra World Trade Centre (PWTC), and the Kuala Lumpur International Convention Centre host international conferences, trade expos, and government summits that require professional event managers with logistics, vendor management, and budget control skills. Beyond MICE, the sector includes consumer events (concerts, festivals, brand activations), government ceremonies (national day events, state functions), and the large Malay wedding (walimatulurus) event industry which has professionalised substantially with dedicated event companies. There is no single mandatory degree, but programmes in Event Management, Hospitality Management, Business Administration, or Mass Communication are common entry routes — Taylor's University, UCSI, and KDU offer specific event management degrees. Internships with event companies in Bangsar South, Bukit Bintang, and Damansara are the most effective route to employment. Starting salaries are modest — RM 2,000–3,000 for event coordinators — but experienced senior event managers running major corporate accounts or large-scale productions can earn RM 6,000–12,000. Freelance event management is also viable, with day rates for experienced practitioners ranging from RM 500 to RM 2,500 depending on the event scale.

Pengurusan acara di Malaysia merangkumi MICE (KLCC, PWTC), acara pengguna, upacara kerajaan, dan industri perkahwinan. Tiada ijazah wajib, tetapi Pengurusan Acara, Hospitaliti, atau Komunikasi Massa adalah laluan biasa. Gaji permulaan RM 2,000–3,000; pengurus acara kanan boleh capai RM 6,000–12,000.
