JPA vs MARA Scholarship 2026 — A Clear Comparison for Malaysian Students

Biasiswa JPA vs MARA 2026 — Perbandingan Jelas untuk Pelajar Malaysia

JPA (Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam) and MARA (Majlis Amanah Rakyat) are the two pillars of Malaysian government scholarship funding — but they are not the same programme and do not target the same students. JPA scholarships are open to all Malaysian citizens regardless of race, though competition is intense and the programme has historically favoured students with very strong SPM results (10A+ or near-equivalent) targeting high-demand courses like medicine, law, engineering, and professional accounting. JPA offers both local and overseas scholarships, with overseas placements to the UK, Australia, Japan, and others. MARA, by contrast, is specifically for Bumiputera students — Malay and indigenous Malaysians — and operates its own network of institutions (MRSM schools, Universiti Teknologi MARA, UiTM) alongside scholarship programmes for study at private and overseas universities. MARA scholarships have less extreme SPM-score cutoffs than JPA and tend to focus on students from lower-income backgrounds. The bond period for both programmes is typically 10 years of government service for overseas placements, shorter for local placements.

JPA terbuka kepada semua warganegara Malaysia manakala MARA khusus untuk pelajar Bumiputera. Kedua-duanya menawarkan biasiswa tempatan dan luar negara dengan tempoh ikatan perkhidmatan awam.
