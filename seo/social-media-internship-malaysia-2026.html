Social Media Internships in Malaysia 2026

Internship Media Sosial Malaysia 2026

Social media internships in Malaysia have expanded significantly as brands have shifted budget from traditional media toward digital. The role of a social media intern varies by company: at a large brand, you are likely supporting a team with scheduling, community management, and analytics reporting. At a small agency or startup, you might be running channels almost independently from week two. The most in-demand skills for these roles in Malaysia are short-form video production (TikTok and Instagram Reels), copywriting in both English and BM, basic graphic design using Canva or Adobe, and familiarity with Meta Business Suite. Mass communications, digital media, and multimedia design students are most commonly placed, though marketing and business students with a demonstrated social media portfolio are equally competitive.
