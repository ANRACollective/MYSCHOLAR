IT Support Internship Malaysia 2026 — Your Entry Point Into Tech

Internship IT Support Malaysia 2026 — Pintu Masuk ke Industri Teknologi

IT support internships are among the most accessible tech-adjacent roles for diploma and TVET students in Malaysia — companies ranging from MSPs (managed service providers) to large corporate IT departments regularly take on interns for helpdesk, hardware support, and network monitoring roles. The work is practical and varied: setting up workstations, responding to user tickets, assisting with software deployment, and supporting network troubleshooting. This hands-on exposure is genuinely valuable for building the practical skills that certifications like CompTIA A+ test on. Major employers in Malaysia that regularly take IT support interns include Telekom Malaysia, Maxis, TNB, EPF, PETRONAS, large banks, and IT services companies like NTT, DXC Technology, and IBM Malaysia. Stipends for IT support internships typically range from RM800 to RM1,500 depending on the employer. TVET students from polytechnics and community colleges are actively recruited — this is one of the more egalitarian corners of the tech internship market.
