Fully Funded Overseas Scholarships for Malaysian Students — 2026 Guide

Biasiswa Luar Negara Fully Funded untuk Pelajar Malaysia 2026

A fully funded overseas scholarship in Malaysia typically covers tuition fees, a monthly living allowance, return flights, and sometimes a book or settling-in allowance — meaning the recipient pays nothing out of pocket. These are the most competitive scholarships in the country and the most transformative for recipients. The main domestic providers are JPA (overseas degree placements to UK, USA, Australia, and other approved countries), MARA (UK, Australia, New Zealand, and selected European countries), Petronas (primarily UK and Australia, tied to engineering and energy-related fields), and Yayasan Khazanah (selective, business and STEM focus, global universities). Foreign government awards are often overlooked by Malaysian students but genuinely accessible: the UK Commonwealth Scholarships, Germany's DAAD, France's Eiffel Excellence Programme, Japan's MEXT scholarship, and the Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) scholarship all accept Malaysian applicants. These foreign awards often have no bond obligation, which makes them attractive despite their competitiveness. The application timelines for foreign government scholarships often open earlier than Malaysian students expect — MEXT applications, for instance, typically close in June for the following year's intake. MyScholar lists overseas and fully funded scholarships filtered separately so you can browse what is realistic for your field and target country.

Biasiswa luar negara fully funded menanggung semua kos — yuran, elaun bulanan, dan penerbangan. Pembekal utama ialah JPA, MARA, Petronas, dan Yayasan Khazanah. Ramai pelajar Malaysia terlepas pandang biasiswa kerajaan asing seperti Commonwealth UK, DAAD Jerman, dan MEXT Jepun — yang juga terbuka untuk pemohon Malaysia.
