Defence and Aerospace Internship in Malaysia 2026 — DEFTECH, AIROD, CTRM, and Boustead Naval

Internship Pertahanan dan Aeroangkasa di Malaysia 2026 — DEFTECH, AIROD, CTRM, dan Boustead Naval

Malaysia's defence and aerospace sector is small by global standards but produces internship opportunities of unusual technical depth that are difficult to replicate in commercial sectors. The major companies in this space — which serve both the Malaysian Armed Forces (ATM) and civilian aviation — offer placements in armoured vehicle systems, aircraft maintenance and modification, composite materials manufacturing, and naval vessel construction. DEFTECH (Defence Technologies Sdn Bhd), a subsidiary of DRB-HICOM, is Malaysia's primary manufacturer of armoured personnel carriers (the AV4 Lipan Bara and related 4x4 vehicles) and military trucks. DEFTECH's engineering team in Pekan, Pahang works on systems integration, vehicle testing, and production — Engineering students (Mechanical, Electrical, Manufacturing) find technically substantive placements here. AIROD (Aircraft Inspection, Repair and Overhaul Services) in Subang is a military and civilian MRO (Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul) facility certified to work on aircraft including the Royal Malaysian Air Force (RMAF) fleet. Aerospace Engineering and Aeronautical Engineering students from UniKL MIAT, UiTM, and UTM compete for AIROD placements. CTRM (Composites Technology Research Malaysia) in Batu Berendam, Melaka specialises in composite material structures for aerospace — manufacturing aircraft components for Airbus, Boeing, and other OEMs. This is a niche but globally connected workplace that offers rare hands-on composite manufacturing experience. Boustead Naval Shipyard in Lumut, Perak constructs and maintains vessels for the Royal Malaysian Navy (RMN) — Marine Engineering, Naval Architecture, and Electrical Engineering students find relevant placements in shipyard operations. Security clearance requirements apply to some placements; Malaysian citizenship is typically required for defence-adjacent internships.

AIROD di Subang: MRO tentera dan awam untuk RMAF — Kejuruteraan Aeroangkasa dari UniKL MIAT, UiTM, UTM. CTRM di Batu Berendam, Melaka: struktur komposit untuk Airbus/Boeing — pengalaman pembuatan komposit global yang jarang. Boustead Naval Shipyard di Lumut, Perak: pembinaan dan penyelenggaraan kapal untuk RMN. Keperluan pelepasan keselamatan dan kewarganegaraan Malaysia lazimnya diperlukan.
