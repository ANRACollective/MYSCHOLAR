Scholarship Scams in Malaysia 2026 — How to Tell What Is Real

Biasiswa Palsu Malaysia 2026 — Cara Kenal Pasti Penipuan

Scholarship scams in Malaysia have increased alongside the growth of TikTok and WhatsApp scholarship groups, where fake or misleading awards are shared without verification. Here is how to tell the difference in 2026. A legitimate scholarship will never ask you to pay a registration fee, processing fee, or insurance premium to apply. Any scholarship that asks for payment before disbursing funds is a scam — no exceptions. Legitimate scholarships are announced through official channels: the sponsor's verified website, official MyMOHE portal (for JPA and public university scholarships), or directly through university financial aid offices. Warning signs to look for: a scholarship with no traceable sponsoring organisation, prizes that seem unusually large for an unknown sponsor, applications that ask for bank account details early in the process, and social media-only scholarships with no official landing page. If you cannot find the scholarship on the sponsor's own website, it is either fake or you are looking at an unofficial re-share with errors.
