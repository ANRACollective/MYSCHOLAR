Scholarships for Low CGPA Students in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa CGPA Rendah Malaysia 2026 — Masih Ada Peluang

The TikTok myth that you cannot get a scholarship without a 3.8 CGPA is wrong. Here is the accurate picture for Malaysia 2026. JPA and MARA full scholarships do require strong results — typically 9A for SPM or first-class honours for degree-level awards. But that is a narrow slice of what is available. Many GLC and corporate scholarships set minimums at 3.0 or 3.2. Some company-sponsored scholarships for specific programmes have no published CGPA floor at all — they are looking for fit and commitment, not grades. State scholarships like Yayasan Johor and Yayasan Sabah have lower thresholds than federal equivalents. Skills-based scholarships in vocational and technical fields (TVET) rarely gate on CGPA. The short version: if your CGPA is 2.8 to 3.2, you probably qualify for more than you think.
