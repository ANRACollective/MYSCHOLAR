UM vs UPM Scholarships 2026 — What Malaysian Students Need to Know

Biasiswa UM vs UPM 2026 — Apa Yang Pelajar Malaysia Perlu Tahu

Choosing between Universiti Malaya and UPM involves more than rankings — scholarship availability is a real factor. UM offers a higher density of internal merit scholarships per faculty, particularly in medicine, law, and engineering. UM's QS ranking also means more international scholarships and exchange programmes specify UM as an eligible institution. UPM has a stronger agricultural and life sciences scholarship landscape, with MARDI, Felda, and Forestry Department scholarships frequently tied to UPM programmes. Both universities are JPA-eligible for public service bonded scholarships. For private company and GLC scholarships, the company often specifies courses rather than universities — so if your intended course is offered at both, the scholarship landscape is identical. The practical difference shows up in faculty-specific awards and research scholarships — UM is stronger in medicine, law, and social science; UPM is stronger in agriculture, veterinary, and environmental science. MyScholar lists scholarships by field so you can filter by course, not just university.

Memilih antara Universiti Malaya dan UPM melibatkan lebih daripada kedudukan — ketersediaan biasiswa adalah faktor sebenar. UM menawarkan kepadatan biasiswa merit dalaman yang lebih tinggi per fakulti, terutamanya dalam perubatan, undang-undang, dan kejuruteraan. UPM mempunyai landskap biasiswa sains pertanian dan hayat yang lebih kukuh, dengan biasiswa MARDI, Felda, dan Jabatan Perhutanan yang sering dikaitkan dengan program UPM.
