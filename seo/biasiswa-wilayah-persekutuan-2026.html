KL & Federal Territory Scholarship 2026

Biasiswa Wilayah Persekutuan 2026

Students from Kuala Lumpur and the Federal Territories can access YAPEIM, community foundation scholarships, and national awards. The federal territory doesn't have a state body like other states, but there are still dedicated funds worth knowing about.

Pelajar dari Kuala Lumpur dan Wilayah Persekutuan boleh mengakses YAPEIM, biasiswa yayasan komuniti, dan anugerah nasional. Wilayah persekutuan tidak mempunyai badan negeri seperti negeri lain, tetapi masih terdapat dana khusus yang patut diketahui.
