Scholarships for Women in Malaysia — 2026 Guide to Female-Specific Awards

Biasiswa Khas untuk Wanita di Malaysia 2026 — Panduan Lengkap

There is a meaningful set of Malaysian scholarships that either exclusively target female candidates or give substantial preference to women — particularly in STEM fields and in leadership-focused awards. These exist for several reasons: addressing the gender gap in certain engineering and technology fields; supporting women from lower-income backgrounds who face additional barriers; and corporate and foundation initiatives focused on women's economic empowerment. Notable scholarships with a women's focus in Malaysia include the Yayasan Sime Darby education fund (which has historically prioritised girls from plantation communities), various state-level Majlis Agama and Yayasan programmes targeted at women pursuing religious education or community development, and a growing number of corporate STEM scholarships where female candidates in engineering and computer science are actively preferred. International scholarships with women-specific tracks accessible to Malaysians include the OWSD (Organization for Women in Science for the Developing World) and several Commonwealth scholarship variants for women in STEM. Beyond women-specific scholarships, the broader Malaysian scholarship landscape does not systematically disadvantage women — female students actually represent the majority of scholarship recipients in many programmes. However, women pursuing non-traditional fields (engineering, technical trades, defence) often find specific women's scholarships available as an additional avenue.
