Scholarships with August 2026 Deadlines — What Is Still Open in Malaysia

Biasiswa dengan Tarikh Tutup Ogos 2026 — Apa yang Masih Dibuka di Malaysia

August is a strategically important month in Malaysia's scholarship calendar because it sits at the intersection of two key student transition moments: the end of the pre-university year for STPM and A-Level students who received results in July, and the start of the September/October university intake for new first-year students. Scholarships with August deadlines typically include late-cycle corporate awards from companies whose intake planning runs on a financial year calendar (September to August), foundation grants for students who have just confirmed their university places, and university-administered merit scholarships that require a confirmed offer letter — something students only receive from July onwards. The August window also captures scholarship opportunities for students who applied earlier in the year but were waitlisted or shortlisted for a secondary cycle. MyScholar lists over 200+ scholarships with live deadline information — using the platform to filter by closing date is the fastest way to identify what is genuinely open in August without reading through dozens of individual scholarship websites. Key categories to check in August: private university merit awards, state government scholarships with September semester alignment, and zakat-funded education grants from state MAIBs (Majlis Agama Islam Negeri) which operate on Islamic calendar cycles.

Ogos adalah bulan penting dalam kalendar biasiswa Malaysia — berlaku pada persimpangan antara pelajar STPM/A-Level yang baru terima keputusan (Julai) dan pengambilan universiti September. Biasiswa tutup Ogos termasuk anugerah korporat kitaran akhir, geran yayasan untuk pelajar yang baru sahkan tempat universiti, dan biasiswa merit universiti swasta.
