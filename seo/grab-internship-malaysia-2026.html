Grab Internship Malaysia 2026

Internship Grab Malaysia 2026

Grab is one of Southeast Asia's largest technology companies and its internship programme is among the most competitive student placements in Malaysia. Grab's KL offices host interns across engineering (backend, data, mobile), product management, business development, and operations functions. Most Grab internships run for 3 to 6 months and are based out of Menara Etiqa in Bangsar. Applications are submitted through Grab's careers portal and intake typically opens twice a year. For engineering roles, expect a coding assessment followed by one to two technical interviews. For product and operations roles, expect a case study and a structured interview. Having some familiarity with Grab's ecosystem — GrabPay, GrabFood, GrabMart, GrabCar — is a genuine advantage since product case questions often draw from it. Stipends are competitive by Malaysian internship standards. MyScholar lists Grab internships when active on their careers page.
