Scholarships for Polytechnic and Diploma Students in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Pelajar Politeknik & Diploma Malaysia 2026

Polytechnic and diploma students in Malaysia have a narrower but real set of scholarship options. MARA's TESP programme includes diploma-level sponsorship, particularly for students at MARA's own polytechnics. JPT (Jabatan Pendidikan Tinggi) under the Education Ministry administers bursary allocations to public polytechnics, distributed based on academic performance. For students looking to continue to a degree after diploma, top-up scholarships are available from some GLCs and corporations — Tenaga Nasional, Petronas, and Telekom Malaysia have historically taken polytechnic-trained students into their sponsorship pipelines for degree completion. State-level foundations (Yayasan Negeri) in Selangor, Johor, and Sabah also fund diploma students at public institutions. The key difference from SPM or STPM scholarship applications: polytechnic scholarship applications typically happen mid-programme, not at entry, so knowing the deadlines is critical. MyScholar lists which scholarships accept diploma applicants directly.

Pelajar politeknik dan diploma di Malaysia mempunyai set pilihan biasiswa yang lebih sempit tetapi nyata. Program TESP MARA merangkumi tajaan peringkat diploma, terutama untuk pelajar di politeknik MARA sendiri. JPT di bawah Kementerian Pendidikan mentadbir peruntukan bursari kepada politeknik awam. Untuk pelajar yang ingin meneruskan ke ijazah selepas diploma, biasiswa tambahan tersedia dari beberapa GLC dan syarikat — TNB, Petronas, dan Telekom Malaysia secara sejarah telah mengambil pelajar terlatih politeknik ke dalam saluran tajaan mereka untuk penyelesaian ijazah.
