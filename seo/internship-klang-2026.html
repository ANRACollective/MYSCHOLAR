Internship in Klang 2026 — Port Klang, Logistics, Shipping, and Manufacturing

Internship di Klang 2026 — Port Klang, Logistik, Perkapalan, dan Pembuatan

Klang and Port Klang form one of Southeast Asia's most significant seaport corridors — Port Klang is Malaysia's busiest container port and the 12th busiest in the world by container throughput, handling the vast majority of Malaysia's import and export traffic. For students interested in logistics, supply chain, shipping, and international trade, an internship in the Klang area provides exposure to port operations, container management, freight forwarding, customs brokerage, and maritime services that is genuinely difficult to replicate in any other Malaysian location. The major logistics and shipping employers in Port Klang include: Westports Malaysia (one of the largest port operators in Southeast Asia), Northport (operated by MMC Port Holdings), and the dozens of freight forwarders, shipping agencies, and customs brokers that cluster around both port facilities. Shipping lines with Malaysian offices (Maersk, COSCO, Evergreen, Yang Ming, and MSC) conduct inland support operations from KL/PJ but often route intern candidates through Port Klang for operational exposure. Beyond shipping, Klang's industrial townships — Meru, Bukit Tinggi, Shah Alam's eastern industrial zones, and the Klang industrial estates — house significant manufacturing including steel and metal fabrication, palm oil processing, automotive parts, and food manufacturing. Students in Logistics Management, International Trade, Maritime Studies (from Universiti Malaysia Terengganu and ALAM, the Maritime Academy), Supply Chain, and Mechanical Engineering find relevant placements here. Allowances for logistics internships: RM 600–900; shipping agency internships: RM 700–1,000.

Majikan logistik utama: Westports Malaysia, Northport (MMC Port Holdings), dan syarikat penghantaran kargo dan broker kastam. Laluan perkapalan (Maersk, COSCO, Evergreen) mengarahkan intern untuk pendedahan operasi di Port Klang. Estet industri Klang (Meru, Bukit Tinggi) menempatkan pembuatan keluli, pemprosesan sawit, dan komponen automotif. Elaun: RM 600–1,000 untuk logistik dan perkapalan.
