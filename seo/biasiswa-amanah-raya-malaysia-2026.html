Amanah Raya Scholarship and Education Funds Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa dan Dana Pendidikan Amanah Raya (ARB) Malaysia 2026

Amanah Raya Berhad (ARB) is Malaysia's government-appointed trustee body, which manages estates, trusts, and waqf (endowment) funds. A portion of the assets under ARB management is channelled into education assistance for Malaysian students — primarily through waqf-based scholarship programmes that operate under Islamic endowment principles. These scholarships are less widely known than JPA or MARA equivalents, which means competition is lower and deserving applicants who miss out on mainstream scholarships sometimes find success here. Eligibility typically requires Malaysian citizenship, Muslim faith (for the waqf-specific funds), financial need demonstration, and a minimum academic threshold that is generally more accessible than top-tier scholarships. ARB also manages several charitable trusts left by individuals and organisations that have specific recipient criteria — for example, some are restricted to students from particular states or studying particular subjects. Checking the ARB website directly for the current intake period is advisable as application windows can be short.
