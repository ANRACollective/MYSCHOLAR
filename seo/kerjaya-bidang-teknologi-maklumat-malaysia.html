IT Career Paths in Malaysia 2026 — Software, Data, Cloud, Cyber, and More

Kerjaya Bidang IT di Malaysia 2026 — Semua Laluan yang Perlu Anda Tahu

Information technology is the broadest and most internally varied career field in Malaysia — and also one of the most in-demand. The mistake many students make is treating IT as a single career when it is actually a family of distinct specialisations with very different day-to-day work, skill requirements, and salary ranges. Software development (writing code for web, mobile, or enterprise systems) is the most visible branch. Data science and analytics (building models and extracting insights from large datasets) has seen explosive demand since 2022. Cloud engineering (designing and managing infrastructure on AWS, Azure, or GCP) commands some of the highest salaries in Malaysian tech. Cybersecurity has been the fastest-growing IT discipline since Bank Negara and the government tightened data protection requirements. IT support and systems administration remain the most accessible entry points. Network engineering underpins everything else. UI/UX design sits at the intersection of technology and design. Each of these paths has a different educational and certification route, different entry-level salary, and different ceiling. MyJourney has individual career pages for most of these roles.

IT bukan satu kerjaya tetapi keluarga pengkhususan yang berbeza: pembangunan perisian, sains data, kejuruteraan awan, keselamatan siber, sokongan IT, dan reka bentuk UX. Setiap satu mempunyai laluan, gaji, dan permintaan yang berbeza.
