Yayasan Kelantan (YAKIN) Scholarship 2026 — For Kelantan Students

Biasiswa Yayasan Kelantan (YAKIN) 2026 — Untuk Pelajar Kelantan

Yayasan Kelantan Darul Naim (YAKIN) is the state foundation of Kelantan responsible for education loans and scholarship programmes for students from Kelantan. YAKIN offers both outright scholarships and interest-free education loans for Kelantan-domiciled students pursuing tertiary education at local and international institutions. The Kelantan state scholarship is an important supplement for students from the east coast states, where household incomes tend to be lower than the national average and access to private scholarship programmes is more limited. For students from Kelantan who are applying for national scholarships (JPA, MARA), applying to YAKIN simultaneously makes strategic sense — some students use YAKIN funding as a bridge before national scholarship disbursements begin, or as a top-up when national scholarship amounts do not fully cover living costs. MyScholar lists Yayasan Kelantan alongside other east coast state scholarships including Yayasan Terengganu and Yayasan Pahang.
