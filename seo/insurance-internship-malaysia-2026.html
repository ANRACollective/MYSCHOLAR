Insurance & Takaful Internship in Malaysia — Where to Apply in 2026

Internship Insurans & Takaful Malaysia 2026 — Tempat Mohon

Insurance internships in Malaysia are far more varied than the sector's staid reputation suggests. Modern Malaysian insurers have active digital product teams, data analytics departments, actuarial divisions, and claims processing units — all of which take interns. The largest life insurers in Malaysia — AIA Malaysia, Great Eastern Life, Prudential Assurance Malaysia, and Manulife — regularly run structured internship programmes, particularly in Kuala Lumpur. On the general insurance side, AXA Affin, Allianz Malaysia, and Zurich Malaysia are among the main employers. The takaful operators — Etiqa Takaful, Syarikat Takaful Malaysia, and Takaful Ikhlas — represent a growing segment with internship openings particularly relevant for students studying Islamic finance. What you work on depends on which team you join: actuarial interns assist with pricing and reserving work; underwriting interns review policy applications; digital and product interns work on apps and customer journeys; data analytics interns clean and analyse claims data. Finance and accounting students are placed in finance and reporting teams. Insurance internship allowances in Malaysia typically range from RM 800–1,500 per month at the larger companies. Most require a minimum CGPA of 3.0 and are based in KL city centre or Petaling Jaya. MyIntern lists insurance internship openings with direct company links.

Internship insurans di Malaysia bukan hanya untuk pelajar kewangan — syarikat besar seperti AIA, Great Eastern, dan Prudential ambil intern dalam pasukan digital, analitik data, dan aktuarial juga. Elaun biasanya RM 800–1,500 sebulan. Peluang takaful juga semakin meningkat di Etiqa dan Syarikat Takaful Malaysia.
