Internship NGO Malaysia 2026

NGO and non-profit internships in Malaysia are a good fit for students from social sciences, public health, law, communications, and development studies who want hands-on sector experience without going into corporate roles. MERCY Malaysia, WWF Malaysia, Save the Children Malaysia, and Women's Aid Organisation are among the local NGOs that regularly take undergraduate and postgraduate interns. UN agencies with a Malaysian presence, including UNHCR in Kuala Lumpur, also run internship programs, though these tend to have higher academic requirements. Most NGO internships are not fully paid, but many offer a monthly living allowance of RM400 to RM700. The working environment is typically smaller and more cross-functional than corporate roles.
