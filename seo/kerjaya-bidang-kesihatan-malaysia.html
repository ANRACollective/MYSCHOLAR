Health Careers in Malaysia — All Pathways in One Place

Kerjaya Bidang Kesihatan Malaysia — Semua Laluan dalam Satu Tempat

Malaysia's healthcare sector is one of the country's largest employers and a consistent source of stable, respected careers. For students finishing SPM with strong science results, the health sector offers more than just medicine and dentistry — there are eight or more distinct health professions, each with its own regulatory body, degree pathway, and career landscape. Doctor (MBBS, MMC registration), Dentist (BDS, DRC registration), Pharmacist (B.Pharm, Pharmacy Board), Nurse (Diploma or B.Nursing, MNB registration), Physiotherapist (B.Physiotherapy, MPT registration), Occupational Therapist, Medical Laboratory Technologist, and Radiographer are all fully regulated allied health professions in Malaysia. Each has a different study length, salary range, work environment, and demand outlook. MyJourney maps each health career individually — you can see the exact SPM requirements, pre-university options, universities, registration bodies, and salary data for each one before you commit to an application.
