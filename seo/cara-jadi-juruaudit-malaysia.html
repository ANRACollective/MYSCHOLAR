How to Become an Auditor in Malaysia — Big 4, Professional Qualifications, and Career Path

Cara Jadi Juruaudit di Malaysia — Big 4, Kelayakan Profesional, dan Laluan Kerjaya

External auditing is one of the most structured and internationally portable graduate careers available in Malaysia, largely because the professional qualification system is clear, well-recognised, and tied to a defined pathway. The audit profession in Malaysia is regulated by the Malaysian Institute of Accountants (MIA) — to sign audit reports as a Licensed Auditor, practitioners must hold MIA membership and a practicing certificate. The two dominant professional qualifications for auditors in Malaysia are ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) and the Malaysian CA (Chartered Accountant) designation from MIA, which can be obtained by completing the ICAEW ACA, CPA Australia, or the Institute of Chartered Accountants in Malaysia (ICAM) pathway. The Big 4 firms — Deloitte, Ernst & Young (EY), KPMG, and PwC — are the largest employers of audit trainees in Malaysia, each recruiting hundreds of graduates annually into their Audit departments in Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, and Johor Bahru. The standard career entry is as an Audit Associate or Graduate Trainee, earning RM 2,800–3,800 fresh out of university. Firms typically sponsor ACCA papers for trainees and provide structured study leave. After 3–5 years as an Associate and Senior Associate, high performers are promoted to Audit Manager (RM 7,000–12,000). Beyond Big 4, the mid-tier firms (BDO, Baker Tilly, Crowe, RSM, Grant Thornton) offer similar pathways at slightly less competitive entry but with faster progression. The Audit Division under the National Audit Department (Jabatan Audit Negara) is a separate public sector route — Pegawai Audit gred W41 starts at RM 2,400–3,400 and performs compliance auditing of government accounts. For students choosing a degree, Accounting and Finance degrees (from local universities or via ACCA's Foundation route) are the standard entry. CGPA requirements for Big 4 are typically 3.2 and above, but ACCA exemptions from a strong accounting degree can accelerate the qualification timeline significantly.

Pengauditan luar di Malaysia dikawal oleh MIA (Malaysian Institute of Accountants). Kelayakan utama: ACCA atau CA Malaysia. Big 4 (Deloitte, EY, KPMG, PwC) mengambil ratusan graduan setiap tahun sebagai Audit Associate (RM 2,800–3,800). Pengurus Audit berpengalaman (3–5 tahun) boleh capai RM 7,000–12,000. Syarikat pertengahan (BDO, Baker Tilly, Crowe) menawarkan laluan serupa dengan perkembangan lebih pantas.
