Internship Interview Dress Code — Malaysia 2026

Panduan Pakaian Temuduga Internship Malaysia 2026

The right dress code for a Malaysian internship interview depends on the company type, not one universal rule. For banks, law firms, government agencies, and large GLCs, full formal is still expected: slacks or skirt, button shirt or blouse, closed-toe shoes. For tech companies, startups, creative agencies, and regional office arms of international brands, smart casual is fine: clean trousers, a neat shirt or blouse, simple shoes. If in doubt, go one level smarter than you think is needed. You can always take off a jacket; you cannot add formality you did not bring. For virtual interviews, dress from the waist up as you would for in-person. When in doubt about a specific company, check their social media or LinkedIn for photos of how their team presents. Dress code yang betul untuk temuduga internship Malaysia bergantung kepada jenis syarikat, bukan satu peraturan universal. Untuk bank, firma undang-undang, agensi kerajaan, dan GLC besar, formal penuh masih dijangka: seluar atau skirt, kemeja butang atau blaus, kasut bertutup. Untuk syarikat teknologi, syarikat permulaan, agensi kreatif, dan cawangan pejabat serantau jenama antarabangsa, smart casual adalah baik. Jika ragu, pakai satu tahap lebih kemas daripada yang anda fikir perlu. Anda boleh tanggal jaket; anda tidak boleh tambah formaliti yang tidak dibawa.
