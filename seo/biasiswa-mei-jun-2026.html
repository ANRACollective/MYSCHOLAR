Scholarships Open in May–June 2026 — What to Apply for After SPM Results

Biasiswa Buka Mei–Jun 2026 — Apa yang Perlu Dimohon Selepas Keputusan SPM

May and June mark one of the busiest scholarship application windows in Malaysia, triggered by the release of SPM results in March and the subsequent rush of applications from school leavers. Government scholarship portals, GLC foundations, and private corporate sponsors typically open their 2026 intake in this window. JPA and MARA applications are usually among the first to open, followed by bank foundations (Maybank, CIMB, RHB), telecommunications companies (Maxis, Celcom, Digi, now merged as CelcomDigi), and industry associations. The advice for students in this window: apply broadly and early. Many scholarships have rolling short-listing rather than a single deadline, which means early applications receive more thorough review. Keep your documents ready — academic transcripts, co-curricular records, a personal statement, and your MyKad. MyScholar tracks deadlines for over 200 scholarships and is updated regularly as new applications open.
