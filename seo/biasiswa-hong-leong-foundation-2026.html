Hong Leong Foundation Scholarship 2026 — Non-Bonded Merit Award, Closing 31 May

Biasiswa Yayasan Hong Leong 2026 — Anugerah Merit Tanpa Ikatan, Tutup 31 Mei

The Hong Leong Foundation Merit Scholarship is one of the few major corporate scholarships in Malaysia that is genuinely non-bonded — recipients have no obligation to work for the Hong Leong Group upon graduation. The scholarship is awarded annually to students pursuing Diploma, Advanced Diploma, or undergraduate degree programmes at recognised public and private institutions in Malaysia, covering any field of study. For undergraduates at public universities, the award is RM 8,500 per annum; for those at local private universities on '3+0' programmes, it reaches RM 24,000 per annum. The application window opens 1 April and closes 31 May each year, making May the last chance to apply for the current cycle. Hong Leong Group is one of Malaysia's largest conglomerates, spanning financial services (Hong Leong Bank, Hong Leong Financial Group), manufacturing, and property — but the scholarship itself carries no sector commitment. This combination of breadth, meaningful award quantum, and no bond makes it one of the most applicant-friendly corporate scholarships available to Malaysian students right now.

Biasiswa Merit Yayasan Hong Leong adalah salah satu biasiswa korporat utama di Malaysia yang benar-benar tanpa ikatan perkhidmatan. Ia terbuka untuk pelajar Diploma dan Ijazah di universiti awam dan swasta, merangkumi semua bidang pengajian. Permohonan dibuka 1 April dan ditutup 31 Mei setiap tahun.
