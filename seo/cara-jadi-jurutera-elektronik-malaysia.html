How to Become an Electronics Engineer in Malaysia — Semiconductors, Circuits, and Career

Cara Jadi Jurutera Elektronik di Malaysia

Malaysia is one of the world's top semiconductor manufacturing hubs, which makes electronics engineering one of the most employment-secure degrees in the country. The Penang corridor alone houses Intel, AMD, Infineon, Osram, and dozens of other global chip and electronics manufacturers — creating consistent demand for electronics engineers at every level from fresh graduate to senior R&D. Electronics engineering covers circuit design, embedded systems, signal processing, power electronics, and increasingly, IoT and wireless systems. The degree is offered at UTM, UPM, UTEM, UTeM, UiTM, Multimedia University, and most major private universities. Accreditation through the Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM) is required for professional registration. Starting salaries for fresh electronics engineers are typically in the RM2,800–RM3,500 range, rising significantly with specialisation in semiconductor process engineering or RF design.

Malaysia adalah hab pembuatan semikonduktor terbesar di Asia Tenggara. Jurutera elektronik mempunyai permintaan tinggi terutama di koridor Pulau Pinang, dengan majikan utama seperti Intel, AMD, dan Infineon.
