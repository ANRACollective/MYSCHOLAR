Telecommunications Internship in Malaysia 2026 — TM, Maxis, CelcomDigi, and the 5G Era

Internship Telekomunikasi di Malaysia 2026 — TM, Maxis, CelcomDigi, dan Era 5G

Malaysia's telecommunications sector is undergoing its most significant transformation in a generation — the national 5G rollout (through Digital Nasional Berhad, DNB, and the subsequent dual-network model), the merger of Celcom and Digi into CelcomDigi, and the aggressive fiber expansion of TM (Telekom Malaysia) through TM ONE and Unifi are creating genuine demand for technical and digital talent at all levels, including interns. The major telco employers for internship placements: TM (Telekom Malaysia), a government-linked company and the largest broadband provider, takes the most interns annually across IT, Network Engineering, Business, Marketing, and HR functions. TM's Cyberjaya headquarters and regional offices are the main sites. Maxis, the premium consumer mobile operator, recruits interns primarily into its digital products, marketing, and IT functions at its Menara Maxis headquarters in KLCC. CelcomDigi — formed from the merger of Celcom Axiata and Digi Telecommunications — has consolidated its operations and internship programme; the merged entity offers placements in network operations, product development, and commercial functions. U Mobile, though smaller, is known in intern circles for a more hands-on placement experience. For students: the most sought-after telco internship specialisations are 5G network planning and optimisation (Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Computer Engineering), cloud infrastructure and DevOps (Computer Science, IT), and digital product management (Business, IT, Communications). Allowances at major telcos: RM 800–1,200 per month — among the higher rates in Malaysia for non-oil-and-gas sectors. Telco internships frequently convert to graduate job offers.

Sektor telekomunikasi Malaysia mengalami transformasi besar — pelancaran 5G (DNB), penggabungan Celcom-Digi menjadi CelcomDigi, dan pengembangan fiber TM. Majikan intern utama: TM (ibu pejabat di Cyberjaya), Maxis (Menara Maxis KLCC), CelcomDigi (operasi disatukan), dan U Mobile. Pengkhususan paling dicari: perancangan rangkaian 5G, infrastruktur awan, dan pengurusan produk digital. Elaun: RM 800–1,200/bulan — antara yang tertinggi dalam sektor bukan minyak & gas. Internship telekomunikasi kerap bertukar kepada tawaran kerja graduan.
