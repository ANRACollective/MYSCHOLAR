Green Technology and Sustainability Scholarships in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Teknologi Hijau dan Kelestarian di Malaysia 2026

Green technology and sustainability scholarships are a growing category in Malaysia's funding landscape, driven by several converging forces: the national Net Zero Carbon target for 2050, Bank Negara Malaysia's Value-Based Intermediation framework for Islamic finance, the Bursa Malaysia sustainability reporting requirements for listed companies, and increasing pressure from international investors for robust ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) credentials. As Malaysia's largest corporations — Petronas, Tenaga Nasional, CIMB, Maybank, Sime Darby, and IOI Group — publish sustainability commitments and establish ESG divisions, some have begun directing scholarship funding toward environmental science, renewable energy engineering, sustainability management, and circular economy disciplines. TNB (Tenaga Nasional Berhad) offers scholarships that include Engineering programmes relevant to Malaysia's electricity transition (solar, hydropower, grid management). Petronas's scholarship programmes, while primarily oil and gas-focused, increasingly include sustainable energy engineering as Malaysia's national energy company navigates the energy transition. The Malaysian Green Technology and Climate Change Corporation (MGTC) under MITI administers government green tech programmes and occasionally supports student training and awards. International scholarships relevant to Malaysian students in this space include: the Chevening Scholarship (UK) which prioritises climate and sustainability research, the British Council Climate Scholarship, and various ASEAN sustainable development fellowships. The field to study for maximum scholarship access: Environmental Engineering, Chemical Engineering with process sustainability specialisation, Renewable Energy Engineering, Environmental Science, and Sustainability Management.

Biasiswa teknologi hijau dan kelestarian semakin berkembang di Malaysia didorong oleh sasaran Net Zero 2050, keperluan ESG Bursa Malaysia, dan tekanan pelabur antarabangsa. TNB menawarkan biasiswa termasuk kejuruteraan peralihan tenaga. Petronas semakin memasukkan kejuruteraan tenaga mampan. MGTC di bawah MITI menyokong program teknologi hijau. Biasiswa antarabangsa berkaitan: Chevening (UK), British Council Climate Scholarship. Bidang terbaik: Kejuruteraan Alam Sekitar, Tenaga Boleh Diperbaharui, dan Pengurusan Kelestarian.
