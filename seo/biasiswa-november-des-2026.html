Scholarships with November and December 2026 Deadlines — What Is Still Open

Biasiswa dengan Tarikh Tutup November dan Disember 2026 — Apa yang Masih Dibuka

November and December represent the final quarter of the scholarship application calendar in Malaysia, and the nature of the awards available shifts meaningfully compared to earlier in the year. The April–June window is dominated by pre-university and fresh undergraduate applications; the November–December window is more relevant to students who are already enrolled and seeking continuation scholarships, second-year merit renewals, or postgraduate funding. Corporate scholarship cycles that run on a financial year (January–December) often have their final intake closing in November or December — awards from companies with October–December year-end planning cycles include several banking sector scholarships and private foundation grants. International scholarship programs with December deadlines relevant to Malaysian students include: Chevening (UK) — applications typically close in early November; Gates Cambridge — deadline in mid-December; DAAD (Germany) — October/November for most programmes; Fulbright (US) — October for the Malaysian-American Commission on Educational Exchange cycle. State scholarship boards — Yayasan Selangor, Yayasan Johor, Yayasan Sabah, and others — sometimes have a second intake in the fourth quarter for continuing students who meet their criteria. Zakat-funded education grants from state religious councils (MAIJ, MAIS, MAINPP, and others) operate on Islamic calendar cycles rather than Gregorian, which means their deadlines do not align predictably with the standard scholarship calendar. MyScholar recommends students use the platform's deadline filter to check what is currently open regardless of month — the database is updated regularly. Students should also check for continuation and renewal deadlines if they are already on partial scholarships.
