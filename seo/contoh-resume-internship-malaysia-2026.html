Internship Resume Examples for Malaysian Students 2026

Contoh Resume Internship Pelajar Malaysia 2026

Most Malaysian students spend more time worrying about their resume than writing it. The truth is, interning employers in Malaysia are not expecting a two-page CV. A clean one-pager with your course, CGPA, relevant coursework, and any co-curriculum involvement gets you shortlisted. What kills applications is a cluttered format, an outdated photo (remove it for international companies, keep it for local ones), or a generic objective statement that says nothing. MyScholar lists internships at companies that actually respond to student applications, so you spend less time applying to dead-end postings.

Ramai pelajar Malaysia habiskan lebih banyak masa risau tentang resume daripada menulis resume itu sendiri. Hakikatnya, majikan internship di Malaysia tidak mengharapkan CV dua muka surat. Satu halaman yang kemas dengan kursus, CGPA, subjek berkaitan, dan penglibatan ko-kurikulum sudah cukup untuk shortlist. Yang menyebabkan permohonan ditolak adalah format yang berselerak, gambar lapuk (buang untuk syarikat antarabangsa, simpan untuk syarikat tempatan), atau ayat objektif generik yang tidak bermakna.
