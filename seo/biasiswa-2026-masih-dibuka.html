Biasiswa Malaysia 2026 Yang Masih Dibuka — Mohon Sekarang

Keeping track of which Malaysian scholarships are currently open is harder than it should be. Scholarship bodies rarely send reminders, application windows are short — often four to six weeks — and many students miss opportunities simply because they did not know a scholarship was open. This is one of the core problems MyScholar was built to solve. The scholarship cycle in Malaysia broadly follows the academic calendar: major government scholarships (JPA, MARA) open after SPM results in February/March. GLC and corporate scholarships like Petronas, Khazanah, and bank scholarships typically open between March and July. Yayasan foundations have varied timelines — some open twice a year, some once. Overseas scholarships from foreign governments (Commonwealth, Eiffel, Fulbright, DAAD) tend to open six to twelve months before the intake they fund, meaning the window is often in September–January for the following academic year. MyScholar tracks deadlines and marks each scholarship as Open, Closing Soon, or Closed so you always know where you stand. The most common missed opportunity: scholarships that open and close in a four-week window between March and May — this is when you need to be watching.

Biasiswa GLC dan korporat pula dibuka antara Mac dan Julai. MyScholar pantau tarikh tutup setiap biasiswa supaya anda tidak terlepas peluang.
