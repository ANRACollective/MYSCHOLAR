Physics Tuition in Petaling Jaya 2026

Tuisyen Fizik Petaling Jaya 2026

Physics is the second most sought-after tuition subject after Add Maths among Form 4 and Form 5 students in Petaling Jaya. PJ has one of the highest concentrations of tuition centres in Malaysia — the SS2, SS15, and Damansara Utama strips in particular have dozens of options within a few kilometres. SPM Physics covers five main modules: Forces and Motion, Heat, Waves, Light and Optics, Electricity and Electromagnetism, and Electronics. Most students struggle with Waves and the quantitative parts of Electricity rather than the conceptual sections. A good Physics tuition centre should work through past-year SPM questions from 2019 to 2025, since the paper structure and question types have been consistent. For IGCSE Physics (0625), the Cambridge approach to experimental design and data analysis differs significantly from SPM — make sure the tutor knows which syllabus they are teaching.
