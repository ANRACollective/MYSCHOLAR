Aviation Internships in Malaysia 2026

Internship Penerbangan Malaysia 2026

Malaysia has a well-developed aviation sector centred around KLIA in Sepang, with AirAsia and Malaysia Airlines as the two major carriers and Malaysia Airports Holdings Berhad (MAHB) managing the airport infrastructure. Internship opportunities in Malaysian aviation span airline operations, revenue management, ground handling, airport retail and lounges, engineering and maintenance, and corporate functions at airline headquarters. AirAsia's RedBeat Ventures arm also offers openings in tech and product for students interested in the digital side of aviation. Most aviation internships require a minimum commitment of 12 to 16 weeks. Students from aviation management, logistics, business, and engineering programmes are most commonly considered.
