E-Commerce Internship Malaysia 2026 — Marketplace Operations, Growth, and Logistics

Internship E-Dagang Malaysia 2026 — Operasi, Pemasaran, dan Logistik

Malaysia is one of Southeast Asia's most active e-commerce markets, and the industry's internship opportunities reflect that scale. Shopee Malaysia, Lazada Malaysia, TikTok Shop, Zalora, and a growing number of D2C (direct-to-consumer) brands all take interns across a wide range of functions. Roles in e-commerce internships typically fall into: seller operations (supporting merchants with listings, logistics, and performance), growth marketing (running campaigns, analysing metrics, A/B testing), category management (managing product assortment in a specific vertical), logistics coordination (working with warehouse and last-mile partners), and product management (working on platform features). For business and marketing students, e-commerce internships offer exposure to real data at scale — millions of transactions, millions of users — which is genuinely hard to get elsewhere. For tech students, the platform engineering and data roles are serious. Shopee and Lazada have structured internship programmes with application cycles typically in December–January for mid-year intake and June–July for year-end intake.

Malaysia adalah salah satu pasaran e-dagang paling aktif di Asia Tenggara. Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop, dan jenama D2C menawarkan internship dalam bidang operasi, pemasaran, pengurusan kategori, dan teknologi.
