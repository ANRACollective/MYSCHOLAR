What to Do After an Internship Rejection — Malaysia 2026

Kena Tolak Internship? Apa Nak Buat Seterusnya 2026

Getting rejected from internship applications in Malaysia is far more common than students expect, and it almost never means what you think it means. Large companies receive hundreds of applications per intake and most rejections happen at the resume screening stage before anyone has read your application in full. If you were rejected after an interview, that is actually a good sign: it means you got further than most. Ask for feedback if you can, some Malaysian companies will give it. The practical next step is to apply to at least ten companies at once rather than waiting for one response before moving to the next. Track your applications in a simple spreadsheet. Widen your target list to include mid-sized local companies which are often more engaged with student applicants than large corporations. MyScholar lists active openings across all company sizes.

Ditolak daripada permohonan internship di Malaysia jauh lebih biasa daripada yang disangka pelajar, dan ia hampir tidak pernah bermakna apa yang anda fikir ia bermakna. Syarikat besar menerima ratusan permohonan setiap pengambilan dan kebanyakan penolakan berlaku di peringkat penyaringan resume sebelum sesiapa membaca permohonan anda sepenuhnya. Jika anda ditolak selepas temuduga, itu sebenarnya tanda yang baik: bermakna anda berjaya melangkah lebih jauh daripada kebanyakan. Minta maklum balas jika boleh. Langkah praktikal seterusnya adalah memohon sekurang-kurangnya sepuluh syarikat sekaligus daripada menunggu satu respons sebelum bergerak ke yang seterusnya.
