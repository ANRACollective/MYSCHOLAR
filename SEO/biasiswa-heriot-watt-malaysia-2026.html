Biasiswa Heriot-Watt University Malaysia 2026

Heriot-Watt University Malaysia is the Malaysian campus of Heriot-Watt University, founded in Edinburgh in 1821 and one of Scotland's most established institutions. The Malaysia campus is located in Putrajaya and offers degrees in engineering, business, computing, and the built environment — all awarded by the UK parent institution and internationally recognised. Heriot-Watt Malaysia offers merit scholarships for high-achieving students, with awards covering partial or full tuition. The university is particularly well-regarded for its engineering and actuarial science programmes, and its graduates benefit from the Scottish university's global alumni network. For students interested in engineering or actuarial careers, Heriot-Watt Malaysia is one of the more targeted scholarship opportunities — fewer students apply compared to larger private universities, making competition somewhat lower for equivalent result bands.
