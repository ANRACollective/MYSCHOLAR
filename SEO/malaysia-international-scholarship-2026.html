Biasiswa Antarabangsa Malaysia (MIS) 2026

The Malaysia International Scholarship (MIS) is KPT's flagship overseas postgraduate award. It covers tuition, a living allowance, and return airfare for masters and PhD candidates at highly ranked universities worldwide. The 2026/2027 application window opened in March — competition is high and essays matter. MyScholar lists MIS alongside every other active scholarship so you can compare return-of-service conditions and decide what fits your plans.

Biasiswa Antarabangsa Malaysia (MIS) adalah anugerah pascasiswazah luar negara utama KPT.
