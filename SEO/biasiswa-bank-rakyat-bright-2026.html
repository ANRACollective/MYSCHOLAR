Bank Rakyat BRIGHT Scholarship 2026 — Malaysia

Biasiswa Bank Rakyat BRIGHT 2026

Bank Rakyat's BRIGHT scholarship programme opened applications in April 2026, continuing its track record as one of the more accessible cooperative banking scholarships in Malaysia. The BRIGHT programme (Building Resilient and Intelligent Global-minded Talent) targets high-performing Bumiputera students at pre-university and undergraduate level, with a preference for finance, economics, business, and STEM fields. The scholarship covers full tuition fees and includes a living allowance, with recipients expected to complete a bond period with Bank Rakyat or its subsidiaries upon graduation. Applicants need a strong SPM or pre-university result, active co-curriculum involvement, and must demonstrate financial need from a family income threshold — Bank Rakyat prioritises B40 and lower-M40 households. The application is submitted online through Bank Rakyat's official scholarship portal.
