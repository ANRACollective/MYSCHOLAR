Biasiswa untuk Pelajar Tahfiz & Huffaz 2026

Tahfiz graduates and huffaz with recognised certificates can apply for dedicated funding programmes in 2026. The Skim Pembiayaan Huffaz (SPH) from JAKIM is one of the primary options — it covers undergraduate and postgraduate studies locally and abroad. MyScholar tracks these alongside other Islamic education funding so you don't have to search multiple sites.
