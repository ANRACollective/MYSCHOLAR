Logistics and Supply Chain Internships in Malaysia 2026

Internship Logistik & Rantaian Bekalan Malaysia 2026

Malaysia's logistics sector is one of the country's largest employers and one of the most underrepresented in student internship searches. Companies like Pos Malaysia, DHL Express, FedEx, Ceva Logistics, and MISC Berhad run structured internship programmes for operations, procurement, and supply chain management students. Port Klang and the Westport-Northport cluster also draw intern applications from maritime logistics and shipping management students. The work differs significantly by company type: 3PL (third-party logistics) players focus on operational efficiency and route optimisation, while in-house supply chain teams at manufacturers focus on inventory, vendor management, and demand forecasting. For students studying Business Administration, Logistics, or Supply Chain Management at UTM, UPM, or UiTM, these internships directly satisfy industrial training requirements. Stipends are typically in the RM700 to RM1,200 range. MyScholar lists active logistics internships when companies post them.
