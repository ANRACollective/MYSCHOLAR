Internship Shopee & Sea Group Malaysia 2026

Sea Group — the parent company of Shopee, Garena, and SeaMoney — runs an active internship programme in Malaysia from its KL and Petaling Jaya offices. Shopee Malaysia is the largest e-commerce platform in the country and its internship positions span engineering (full-stack, data engineering, ML), product management, growth and marketing, logistics operations, and seller acquisition. Garena, Sea's gaming arm, hires interns for game operations, esports, and marketing roles. The application process across Sea Group starts on their global careers portal, with separate postings for Malaysia. Most technical roles require a coding screen (Hackerrank or similar). For business and marketing roles, expect case studies related to e-commerce or marketplace dynamics. Sea Group internship stipends are among the higher ranges in Malaysia's tech sector.
