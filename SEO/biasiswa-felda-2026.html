FELDA Scholarship Malaysia 2026 — For Children of FELDA Settlers

Biasiswa FELDA Malaysia 2026 — Untuk Anak Peneroka

The FELDA Scholarship Programme is specifically designed for children of FELDA (Federal Land Development Authority) settlers — families living in FELDA land schemes across Malaysia, predominantly in Peninsular Malaysia's agricultural states including Pahang, Johor, Negeri Sembilan, and Terengganu. FELDA is one of Malaysia's oldest and largest rural development agencies, and the scholarship reflects its commitment to the education of the second and third generation of settler families. The scholarship covers undergraduate study at local public or private universities. Eligible students are children or grandchildren of registered FELDA settlers, and applications typically require proof of settler status, SPM or equivalent results, and evidence of household income. The FELDA scholarship is a meaningful option that is often overlooked because it is not widely publicised outside FELDA communities — if you qualify based on settler background, it is worth applying alongside other scholarships. MyScholar lists FELDA alongside other government-linked scholarships for rural and agricultural community students.
