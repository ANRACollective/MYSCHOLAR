IsDB Scholarship Malaysia 2026 — Islamic Development Bank

Biasiswa IsDB Malaysia 2026 — Bank Pembangunan Islam

The Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) Human Capital Development Programme offers scholarships for students from IsDB member countries — which includes Malaysia — to pursue undergraduate or postgraduate study in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and health sciences. For Malaysian Muslim students, the IsDB scholarship is a high-value but lower-awareness opportunity. The scholarship is fully funded and covers tuition, living allowance, travel, and health insurance. Unlike many Malaysian government scholarships, the IsDB scholarship does not require a service bond — it is a grant, not a bonded award. The scholarship is competitive internationally across all 57 IsDB member countries, but Malaysian applicants have a relatively strong track record. The IsDB prefers applicants who intend to contribute to the development of their home country, so strong application essays should connect your field of study to Malaysia's development goals. Applications typically open between September and November. MyScholar lists the IsDB alongside other international Islamic and development bank scholarships.
