About This Scholarship

MARA Tertiary Education Sponsorship (TESP) supports bumiputera students pursuing diploma and degree programmes at MARA-affiliated institutions. A key pathway for students entering polytechnics, community colleges, and MARA universities.

This scholarship is listed and regularly verified on MyScholar — Malaysia's free scholarship directory. We track application windows, eligibility requirements, and deadline changes so you don't miss your window.
