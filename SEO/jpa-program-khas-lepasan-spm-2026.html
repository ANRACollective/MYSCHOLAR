About This Scholarship

The JPA Program Khas Lepasan SPM is one of Malaysia's most competitive government scholarships for SPM leavers seeking local undergraduate study. Offered by Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam (JPA), it covers tuition, living allowance, and book allowance for selected public universities.
