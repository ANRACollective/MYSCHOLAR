Scholarships for B40 Students 2026

Biasiswa Pelajar B40 2026

If your family's household income qualifies as B40, there are specific scholarship streams set aside — particularly through Zakat funds, JPA hardship awards, and state welfare bursaries. These are less competitive than general scholarships and worth applying for first.

Jika pendapatan isi rumah keluarga anda layak sebagai B40, terdapat aliran biasiswa khusus yang diperuntukkan — terutamanya melalui dana Zakat, anugerah kesusahan JPA, dan bursari kebajikan negeri. Ini kurang bersaing berbanding biasiswa umum dan patut dipohon dahulu.
