Gates Cambridge Scholarship for Malaysian Students 2026

Biasiswa Gates Cambridge untuk Pelajar Malaysia 2026

The Gates Cambridge Scholarship is one of the world's most prestigious postgraduate awards, funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and administered by the University of Cambridge. The scholarship is fully funded and covers the full cost of study at Cambridge for any postgraduate programme — master's, PhD, or second undergraduate degree. Malaysian scholars have been selected in previous years, and the country has a small but established community of Gates Cambridge alumni. The scholarship does not target any particular field — scholars have studied everything from molecular biology to public policy to arts and humanities. What the Gates Cambridge selection committee looks for is a combination of outstanding academic achievement, leadership potential, a commitment to improving the lives of others, and a strong fit with Cambridge's research environment. The application process is embedded within the Cambridge admissions process — you apply to Cambridge and indicate your interest in Gates Cambridge funding. For Malaysian applicants, the key differentiator is typically the personal leadership narrative and the quality of the research proposal for PhD applications. This is not a scholarship you apply for with grades alone. MyScholar lists Gates Cambridge alongside other elite international scholarships.
