Internship Application Email Guide — Malaysia 2026

Cara Email Mohon Internship Malaysia 2026

Most Malaysian students apply through portals, but a direct email to HR or a hiring manager still works, especially at mid-sized companies and agencies that do not post publicly. Your subject line should be specific: 'Internship Application — Finance, UTM Final Year, June 2026 Intake' tells them everything they need at a glance. Attach your resume as a PDF. In the body, write three sentences max: who you are, what you can bring, and when you are available. Do not paste your full resume into the email. Follow up once after ten working days if you hear nothing. Beyond that, move on. MyScholar lists companies that actively take interns so you are not guessing.

Kebanyakan pelajar Malaysia mohon melalui portal, tetapi emel terus kepada HR atau pengurus pengambilan masih berkesan, terutama di syarikat bersaiz sederhana dan agensi yang tidak mengiklankan secara awam. Tajuk emel anda perlu spesifik: 'Permohonan Internship, Kewangan, UTM Tahun Akhir, Pengambilan Jun 2026' memberitahu mereka semua yang perlu diketahui sekilas pandang. Lampirkan resume anda sebagai PDF. Dalam badan emel, tulis tiga ayat sahaja: siapa anda, apa yang anda boleh sumbangkan, dan bila anda tersedia. Jangan tampal resume penuh dalam emel. Follow-up sekali selepas sepuluh hari bekerja jika tiada respons.
