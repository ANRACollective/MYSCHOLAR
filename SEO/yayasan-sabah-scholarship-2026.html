Yayasan Sabah Scholarships 2026

Biasiswa Yayasan Sabah 2026

Yayasan Sabah is the state's primary education foundation and one of the longer-running scholarship bodies in East Malaysia. Unlike the general Sabah state scholarship, Yayasan Sabah's programmes are more targeted — covering specific fields and institutions, with a focus on developing talent that returns to contribute to Sabah's economy. Eligible students are typically those with Sabah origins studying at recognised local and overseas universities. MyScholar lists Yayasan Sabah awards alongside other state and federal options.

Yayasan Sabah adalah yayasan pendidikan utama negeri dan salah satu badan biasiswa yang paling lama beroperasi di Malaysia Timur. Tidak seperti biasiswa negeri Sabah yang am, program Yayasan Sabah lebih tersasar — merangkumi bidang dan institusi tertentu, dengan fokus membangunkan bakat yang kembali menyumbang kepada ekonomi Sabah. Pelajar yang layak biasanya mereka yang berketurunan Sabah belajar di universiti tempatan dan luar negara yang diiktiraf.
