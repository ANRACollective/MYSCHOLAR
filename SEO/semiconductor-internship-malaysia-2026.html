Semiconductor Internships in Malaysia

Internship Semikonduktor di Malaysia

Malaysia is one of Southeast Asia's most important semiconductor hubs — Intel, NXP, Qualcomm, Infineon, Keysight, and Renesas all run structured internship programmes here, most of them in Penang. If you're studying electronics or electrical engineering, these companies offer the most technically rigorous industrial training available in Malaysia.

Malaysia adalah salah satu hab semikonduktor paling penting di Asia Tenggara — Intel, NXP, Qualcomm, Infineon, Keysight, dan Renesas semuanya menjalankan program latihan industri berstruktur di sini, kebanyakannya di Penang. Jika anda belajar kejuruteraan elektronik atau elektrik, syarikat-syarikat ini menawarkan latihan industri yang paling teknikal di Malaysia.
