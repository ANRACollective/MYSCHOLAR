Biasiswa untuk Pelajar Malaysia Bukan Bumiputera 2026

The most common question non-Bumiputera Malaysian students have about scholarships is: what can I actually apply for? The honest answer is that the options are meaningful — they just require knowing where to look. JPA full scholarships are open to all races, though in practice the majority of awards go to Bumiputera applicants. Corporate and GLC scholarships from banks, MNCs, and private companies are almost all open to all Malaysian citizens regardless of race — Maybank, CIMB, Petronas corporate awards, Shell, Standard Chartered, RHB, CIMB ASEAN, and most foundation scholarships have no race restriction. Foundation scholarships from Kuok Foundation, Jeffrey Cheah Foundation, YTL Foundation, and Khazanah are all open to all races. Community-specific scholarships from MCA (Chinese), MIC (Indian), and Dong Zong (Chinese education) are available for specific communities. The Tun Hussein Onn National Unity Scholarship is specifically designed to be open to all races. Private university scholarships from Taylor's, Sunway, Monash, APU, and UTAR have no race restriction. MyScholar lets you filter by 'open to all' to see the full non-restricted list in one view.

Soalan paling biasa yang ditanya oleh pelajar Malaysia bukan Bumiputera tentang biasiswa adalah: apa yang sebenarnya boleh saya mohon? Jawapan yang jujur adalah bahawa pilihan adalah bermakna — ia hanya memerlukan mengetahui di mana perlu mencari. Biasiswa penuh JPA terbuka untuk semua kaum, walaupun dalam amalan majoriti anugerah diberikan kepada pemohon Bumiputera.
