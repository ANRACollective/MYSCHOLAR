Scholarship vs PTPTN — Which Is Better in 2026?

Biasiswa vs PTPTN — Mana Lebih Baik pada 2026?

Most students face this question at some point: try for a scholarship, take PTPTN, or both? A scholarship is free money with no repayment — but competition is real and getting one isn't guaranteed. PTPTN is available to almost all students but requires repayment after graduation. The practical answer depends on your results, course, and how risk-averse you are. MyScholar helps you explore what scholarships you're eligible for so you can decide with actual options in front of you.
