The YTL Foundation Scholarship Programme is one of Malaysia's established corporate scholarship awards, funded by the YTL Group — a conglomerate spanning utilities, construction, cement, hotels, and technology. The scholarship targets high-achieving Malaysian students pursuing degrees in fields aligned with YTL's business interests, including engineering, business, and technology. The award typically covers tuition fees and a living allowance, and may include a placement opportunity within YTL companies upon graduation. YTL has a diverse business portfolio across Malaysia and internationally, which means scholars have access to a wide variety of career pathways post-award. Application periods vary by year — in 2026, check the YTL Foundation website directly for the current window.
