Teach For Malaysia Fellowship 2026 — Full Guide

Fellowship Teach For Malaysia 2026 — Panduan Lengkap

Teach For Malaysia is not a scholarship in the traditional sense — it is a two-year paid fellowship where graduates teach in underserved Malaysian schools while developing as leaders. It is included in MyScholar because it is a competitive, funded programme that many Malaysian graduates treat as an alternative to a conventional first job or postgraduate study. Fellows receive a monthly stipend (not a salary from a permanent employer), professional development training, a community of peers, and alumni network access that has proven valuable for careers in public policy, social enterprise, education, and the private sector. The TFM fellowship is one of Malaysia's most competitive graduate programmes — acceptance rates are in the single digits. Applicants are evaluated on leadership potential, resilience, and a genuine commitment to educational equity, not just academic results. You do not need a teaching degree to apply. Most fellows are fresh graduates, though mid-career applicants are accepted. Applications typically open in October and January. MyScholar lists TFM alongside other funded graduate opportunities.
