Creative Internship Portfolio Guide — Malaysia 2026

Cara Buat Portfolio untuk Internship Kreatif 2026

For design, UX, media, and content internships in Malaysia, a portfolio matters more than your CGPA. The good news is that it does not have to contain paid client work. Assignment briefs, personal projects, class work, and self-initiated redesigns of real products all count. Put your three strongest pieces first. Write one paragraph per piece explaining the brief, your decision, and what you would do differently. Host it on Behance, a Notion page, or a simple website. A Canva-designed PDF also works. Malaysian creative studios and agencies shortlist candidates based on portfolio quality before they ever look at academic results. MyScholar lists creative internship openings across Malaysia.

Untuk internship reka bentuk, UX, media, dan kandungan di Malaysia, portfolio lebih penting daripada CGPA anda. Berita baiknya ialah ia tidak semestinya mengandungi kerja klien berbayar. Tugasan, projek peribadi, kerja kelas, dan reka bentuk semula produk sebenar secara sukarela semuanya dikira. Letakkan tiga karya terbaik anda dahulu. Tulis satu perenggan bagi setiap karya yang menjelaskan tugasan, keputusan anda, dan apa yang anda akan buat dengan berbeza. Hos di Behance, halaman Notion, atau laman web ringkas. PDF reka bentuk Canva juga boleh diterima.
