Technical Internship Interview Guide — IT Malaysia 2026

Panduan Temuduga Teknikal Internship IT Malaysia 2026

Malaysian tech companies at the internship level are not looking for senior engineers. They are looking for students who can think through a problem and communicate their reasoning. Most technical screens at companies like Grab, Shopee, or regional tech offices in Malaysia involve a one-hour coding test using HackerRank or a shared editor, followed by a thirty-minute discussion. Brush up on arrays, hashmaps, basic sorting, and one graph traversal algorithm. Be able to walk through your solution step by step as you write it. If you cannot solve the problem, explain what you are trying and why. Malaysian tech hiring managers consistently say that a student who thinks out loud and gets a partial solution is more hireable than one who stays silent and submits a full solution at the last second.
