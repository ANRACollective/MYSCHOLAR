Teaching and Education Internships in Malaysia 2026

Internship Perguruan dan Pendidikan Malaysia 2026

Education internships in Malaysia take two distinct forms. The first is the teaching practicum, a compulsory placement for students at Institut Pendidikan Guru (IPG) and university education faculties, typically spanning 12 to 16 weeks in a government school under the mentorship of a senior teacher. The second is the growing category of education-sector internships at private schools, international schools, tutoring centres, and ed-tech companies. Private and international schools in the Klang Valley, Penang, and Johor Bahru regularly bring in interns for teaching support, curriculum development, and student engagement roles. Ed-tech companies like companies building e-learning platforms also hire interns from education, psychology, design, and tech backgrounds. MyScholar lists education and tutoring-related internship openings across Malaysia.

Internship pendidikan di Malaysia mengambil dua bentuk yang berbeza. Pertama adalah praktikum pengajaran, penempatan wajib untuk pelajar di Institut Pendidikan Guru (IPG) dan fakulti pendidikan universiti, biasanya berlangsung 12 hingga 16 minggu di sekolah kerajaan di bawah bimbingan guru kanan. Kedua adalah kategori internship sektor pendidikan yang semakin berkembang di sekolah swasta, sekolah antarabangsa, pusat tuisyen, dan syarikat ed-tech. Sekolah swasta dan antarabangsa di Lembah Klang, Pulau Pinang, dan Johor Bahru kerap membawa intern untuk sokongan pengajaran, pembangunan kurikulum, dan peranan penglibatan pelajar.
