Scholarships for SPM Leavers in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Lepasan SPM 2026 — Panduan Lengkap

SPM results are out and the scholarship window for 2026 SPM leavers runs roughly from March to July. After July, most scholarships shift to university-level (degree) applicants for January 2027 intake. The key decision you need to make first is foundation vs. matriculation vs. diploma — because different scholarships fund different pathways, and some scholarships require you to take specific pre-university programmes. JPA's full scholarship covers foundation at premier universities. MARA's scholarship funds matriculation and diploma at MARA institutions. Bank scholarships typically cover any accredited foundation programme. If your results are in the 5A to 8A range, the GLC and state scholarship options are stronger than people assume — they are less competitive because fewer students know to apply. MyScholar lists scholarships by eligibility so you can filter by your result tier.

Keputusan SPM telah keluar dan tetingkap biasiswa untuk lepasan SPM 2026 berjalan kira-kira dari Mac hingga Julai. Selepas Julai, kebanyakan biasiswa beralih kepada pemohon peringkat universiti (ijazah) untuk pengambilan Januari 2027. Keputusan utama yang perlu anda buat dahulu adalah asasi vs. matrikulasi vs. diploma — kerana biasiswa yang berbeza membiayai laluan yang berbeza, dan sesetengah biasiswa memerlukan anda mengambil program pra-universiti tertentu.
