Monash University Malaysia Scholarships 2026

Biasiswa Monash University Malaysia 2026

Monash University Malaysia (MUM) is the Malaysian campus of Australia's Monash University, located in Subang Jaya, Selangor. It offers a range of scholarships for domestic and international students, with merit-based awards being the primary funding pathway for Malaysian students. The main scholarship categories at Monash Malaysia in 2026 include the Vice-Chancellor's Scholarship (full tuition for exceptional SPM or equivalent results), the Monash University Malaysia Merit Scholarship (partial tuition for strong but not exceptional results), the Jeffrey Cheah Foundation Scholarship (administered through Sunway-Monash partnerships), and faculty-specific bursaries. For Malaysian students, a Monash Malaysia degree is equivalent in qualification to a degree from the Melbourne campus — the same Monash degree, earned in Malaysia at Malaysian tuition rates, which are significantly lower than overseas fees. Scholarships are typically awarded at point of enrollment, and the application is often embedded in the admissions process. MyScholar lists Monash Malaysia scholarships with their current status.
