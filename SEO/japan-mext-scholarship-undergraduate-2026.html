About This Scholarship

The Japan MEXT Scholarship (Monbukagakusho) for undergraduate students covers full tuition, living allowance, and return airfare for Malaysian students to study in Japan. One of the most comprehensive international scholarships available from the Japanese government via the Embassy of Japan.
