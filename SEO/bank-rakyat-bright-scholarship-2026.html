Bank Rakyat BRIGHT Scholarship 2026

Biasiswa BRIGHT Bank Rakyat 2026

Bank Rakyat's BRIGHT programme — Inspirasi Bank Rakyat Memacu Bakat Tertinggi — is one of the Islamic banking sector's more generous scholarship offerings. The 2026 application opened in April and targets high-achieving SPM and STPM graduates pursuing degrees in finance, banking, accounting, and related fields. It includes a full tuition coverage and a monthly allowance, with a structured placement pathway at Bank Rakyat post-graduation. MyScholar lists BRIGHT alongside other banking scholarships so you can compare conditions before applying.
