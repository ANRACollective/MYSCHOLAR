Scholarships for Children of Single Parents in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Anak Ibu Tunggal Malaysia 2026

Children from single-parent households in Malaysia have access to several funding streams that mainstream scholarship listings often miss. Zakat bodies — Lembaga Zakat Selangor, Zakat Melaka, Majlis Agama Islam Wilayah — give priority to children of single mothers, especially in Muslim households. Yayasan Basmi Kemiskinan and state welfare departments (Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat) offer bursaries for students in verified low-income single-parent situations. PTPTN loans are available to all eligible students regardless of family structure, and single-parent households often qualify for income-based exemptions on repayment. Several state governments — Selangor, Penang, and Johor — run targeted education grants for single-parent families that are separate from national scholarship programmes. MyScholar lists welfare and zakat-linked scholarships in addition to the standard corporate and government ones.

Anak-anak dari keluarga ibu tunggal di Malaysia mempunyai akses kepada beberapa aliran pembiayaan yang senarai biasiswa arus perdana sering terlepas pandang. Badan zakat — Lembaga Zakat Selangor, Zakat Melaka, Majlis Agama Islam Wilayah — memberi keutamaan kepada anak ibu tunggal, terutama dalam keluarga Islam. Yayasan Basmi Kemiskinan dan jabatan kebajikan negeri menawarkan bursari untuk pelajar dalam situasi ibu tunggal berpendapatan rendah yang disahkan. Beberapa kerajaan negeri — Selangor, Pulau Pinang, dan Johor — menjalankan geran pendidikan bertarget untuk keluarga ibu tunggal yang berasingan dari program biasiswa nasional.
