Biasiswa Curtin Malaysia 2026 — Miri, Sarawak

Curtin University Malaysia is located in Miri, Sarawak, and is the Malaysian campus of Curtin University, one of Australia's largest and most internationally recognised universities. As one of Sarawak's premier international university campuses, Curtin Malaysia draws students from across Malaysia and the region, with a strong following among East Malaysian students who prefer to study closer to home at an internationally accredited institution. Curtin Malaysia offers merit scholarships that cover partial or full tuition fees for high-achieving students, including the Vice-Chancellor's Scholarship, the Curtin Malaysia Merit Scholarship, and several faculty-specific awards. For students from Sabah and Sarawak specifically, Curtin Malaysia is worth prioritising because it offers Australian-standard qualifications at Malaysian prices, with strong links to Sarawak's oil and gas, engineering, and resources sector employers. Curtin is one of the universities most frequently cited by petroleum engineering and resources students in East Malaysia. MyScholar lists Curtin Malaysia's scholarship awards with current status.
