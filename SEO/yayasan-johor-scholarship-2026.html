Yayasan Johor Scholarships 2026

Biasiswa Yayasan Johor 2026

Yayasan Johor has historically been one of the more active state foundations, particularly in supporting students from the Johor Bahru corridor into technical and professional degrees. With Johor's economy expanding rapidly through the Johor-Singapore Special Economic Zone, there is increasing corporate co-funding alongside the foundation's own programmes — making it one of the more interesting state-level ecosystems for scholarship seekers. MyScholar tracks all Johor-linked scholarships so students from JB, Batu Pahat, and across the state can compare their options.

Yayasan Johor secara sejarah adalah salah satu yayasan negeri yang paling aktif, terutama dalam menyokong pelajar dari koridor Johor Bahru ke dalam ijazah teknikal dan profesional. Dengan ekonomi Johor berkembang pesat melalui Zon Ekonomi Khas Johor-Singapura, terdapat semakin banyak pembiayaan bersama korporat di samping program yayasan sendiri.
