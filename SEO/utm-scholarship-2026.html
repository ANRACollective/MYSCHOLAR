UTM Scholarships in Malaysia 2026 — Full Guide for Students

Biasiswa UTM 2026 — Panduan Lengkap Untuk Pelajar

UTM (Universiti Teknologi Malaysia) students have strong access to engineering and technology sector scholarships that are specifically targeted at the programmes UTM is known for. The main pipelines to know about in 2026: JPA engineering scholarships that place students at UTM for specific programmes, Yayasan TNB scholarships for electrical and energy-related engineering, Petronas Technical Scholarship for petroleum and chemical engineering, company-sponsored schemes from firms like Samsung, Gamuda, Sunway Construction, and IJM that specifically recruit UTM engineering graduates, and UTM's own internal merit scholarships. The Johor vs. KL campus distinction also matters — some state scholarships are tied to campus location, and Yayasan Johor scholarships are naturally more accessible to students at the Johor campus. For postgraduate students, UTM's research grants and MOSTI-funded scholarships are significant. MyScholar lists UTM-eligible scholarships filtered by programme so engineering students can find what applies to their specific field.

Pelajar UTM (Universiti Teknologi Malaysia) mempunyai akses yang kukuh kepada biasiswa sektor kejuruteraan dan teknologi yang disasarkan khusus kepada program yang UTM terkenal. Saluran utama yang perlu diketahui pada 2026: biasiswa kejuruteraan JPA yang menempatkan pelajar di UTM untuk program tertentu, biasiswa Yayasan TNB untuk kejuruteraan elektrik dan tenaga, Biasiswa Teknikal Petronas untuk kejuruteraan petroleum dan kimia.
