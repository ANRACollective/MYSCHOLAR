Pusat Tuisyen Setiawangsa Kuala Lumpur 2026

Setiawangsa sits on the northeastern edge of central KL, bordered by Wangsa Maju and Keramat. The area has a mix of government quarters, older terrace housing, and newer condominiums — a residential profile that supports a steady demand for tuition, particularly at primary and Form 1 to 3 level. Most tuition activity in Setiawangsa runs through small independent centres and home tutor networks rather than chain centres, which means class sizes are smaller and fees are generally lower than the PJ or Cheras equivalent. Subject demand follows the national pattern: Bahasa Malaysia, Mathematics, Science, and English are the core. For Form 4 and 5 students, Add Maths and Chemistry draw the most demand.
