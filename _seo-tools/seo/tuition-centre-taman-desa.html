Tuition Centres in Taman Desa, Kuala Lumpur

Pusat Tuisyen di Taman Desa, Kuala Lumpur

Taman Desa is a mid-size residential area in south Kuala Lumpur, situated between Old Klang Road and the Kuchai corridor. The area has a mixed population of working families and its tuition scene reflects a practical, results-focused demand — the main subjects sought at Taman Desa centres are SPM Add Maths, Chemistry, Physics, and Bahasa Malaysia. There is also demand for Mandarin tuition given the Chinese school population in the area. The Old Klang Road corridor connects Taman Desa to OUG, Kuchai Lama, and Salak South, and families often compare centres across this stretch. Taman Desa does not have an LRT station but is accessible by feeder buses from Kerinchi station.
