Semiconductor Internships in Penang 2026 — Malaysia's Silicon Island

Internship Semikonduktor di Pulau Pinang 2026

Penang's Bayan Lepas Free Industrial Zone has been Malaysia's semiconductor heartland since the 1970s, and today it is home to some of the most significant chip manufacturing and test operations in Southeast Asia. Intel's Penang campus is the company's largest assembly and test site outside the United States. Infineon, Bosch Automotive, Osram, Globetronics, and Unisem also maintain significant operations in the area. Internship roles in Penang semiconductor companies fall into three main categories: process engineering, quality assurance, and test engineering. Most positions are open to electrical, electronics, materials science, and mechatronics students. The working culture is structured and technical. Applications typically go through company career portals and some via campus recruitment at USM, UniMAP, and Penang polytechnics. MyScholar tracks semiconductor internship openings in Penang.
