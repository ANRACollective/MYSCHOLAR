PhD Scholarships in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa PhD di Malaysia 2026

Doctoral funding in Malaysia runs through a few main channels. MyBrainSc (S3) is the government's primary route for PhD candidates at local public universities — it covers fees and a monthly allowance. Academics pursuing a PhD while on the government payroll can apply under the SLAI scheme. Beyond that, the Iskandar Malaysia Scholars programme and a small number of institutional scholarships at UM, UTM, and UKM offer competitive doctoral awards. MyScholar tracks all current options so you can focus on your application, not the search.

Pensyarah yang mengejar PhD semasa berkhidmat dengan kerajaan boleh memohon di bawah skim SLAI. Selain itu, program Iskandar Malaysia Scholars dan beberapa biasiswa institusi di UM, UTM, dan UKM menawarkan anugerah doktoral yang kompetitif.
