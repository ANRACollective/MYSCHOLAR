Internships in Kuala Lumpur 2026 — Where to Look and Who Is Hiring

Internship Kuala Lumpur 2026 — Di Mana Nak Cari dan Siapa Yang Mengambil

Kuala Lumpur concentrates the majority of Malaysia's internship opportunities, especially in finance, technology, consulting, media, and professional services. The geographic clusters that matter in 2026: KLCC and the city centre (financial services, big law firms, consulting, MNC regional offices), KL Sentral and Bangsar South (tech companies, telcos, shared service centres), Petaling Jaya especially PJ Old Town and Damansara (media companies, agencies, GLCs), and Cyberjaya (technology, outsourcing, startup ecosystem). For students without a car, the LRT, MRT, and KTM coverage makes internships in the KLCC, KL Sentral, and Bangsar South corridors the most accessible. The companies hiring interns most consistently in KL in 2026 across all sectors include the Big 4 accounting firms (all have structured intern programmes), major banks (Maybank, CIMB, RHB, Public Bank), Petronas, Telekom Malaysia, and the larger tech companies with KL offices.
