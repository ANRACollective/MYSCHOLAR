How to Write an Internship Cover Letter in Malaysia 2026

Cara Tulis Cover Letter Internship Malaysia 2026

Most Malaysian students either do not send a cover letter at all or send a version so generic that it hurts rather than helps. Here is what a good internship cover letter looks like in Malaysia in 2026. Keep it to one page — three to four short paragraphs. The first paragraph should state clearly who you are, what you are applying for, and where you heard about the opportunity. The second paragraph should explain why this specific company interests you — this is where most students fail, writing 'I want to gain experience' instead of showing they know something real about the company. The third paragraph should briefly highlight one or two relevant skills or experiences from your academic or co-curricular record that are directly relevant to the role. The fourth paragraph is a short close with your availability dates, your contact, and a polite request for an interview. In Malaysia, the cover letter is often read after the resume — it is a filter for communication skills and company fit. Sending a cover letter at all puts you ahead of most applicants who skip it.
