Video Internship Interview Tips — Malaysia 2026

Tips Temuduga Video Internship Malaysia 2026

Most first-round Malaysian internship interviews are now conducted over Zoom or Microsoft Teams, and the technical side trips up more candidates than the questions do. The night before: test your camera and microphone, check your internet stability, and choose a background that is clean and neutral. A plain wall works better than a virtual background which can glitch. Frame your camera at eye level, not looking up from a laptop on a table. Dress from the waist up as you would for a physical interview. Turn off phone notifications. If your internet drops, stay calm, rejoin, and briefly apologise before continuing. Most Malaysian interviewers expect occasional connectivity issues and it will not count against you if you handle it professionally. MyScholar lists internships with companies across Malaysia.
