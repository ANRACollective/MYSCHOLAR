Scholarships for IGCSE Graduates in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa untuk Lepasan IGCSE di Malaysia 2026

IGCSE and Cambridge O-Level graduates in Malaysia are eligible for most major scholarships — including JPA's PPN programme, Khazanah, and the majority of corporate awards. The key difference is the conversion: most sponsors require you to express your results in terms of A grades, and some have specific grade thresholds that differ from SPM criteria. University endowment scholarships and private bank programmes tend to be the most accessible for IGCSE students. MyScholar lists all scholarships that explicitly accept IGCSE or international curriculum qualifications.

Graduan IGCSE dan Cambridge O-Level di Malaysia layak untuk kebanyakan biasiswa utama — termasuk program PPN JPA, Khazanah, dan majoriti anugerah korporat. Perbezaan utama adalah pada penukaran: kebanyakan penaja memerlukan anda menyatakan keputusan anda dari segi gred A, dan ada yang mempunyai ambang gred khusus yang berbeza dari kriteria SPM. Biasiswa endowmen universiti dan program bank swasta cenderung menjadi yang paling mudah diakses untuk pelajar IGCSE.
