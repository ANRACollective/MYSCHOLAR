Scholarships for New University Students in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Pelajar Baru Universiti Malaysia 2026

If you are just starting university in Malaysia in 2026, the scholarship window you have right now is different from the one SPM leavers have. You can no longer apply for scholarships that require pre-university or SPM result applications — those windows are typically January to July. What you can still access as a new student: university internal merit scholarships (many are awarded in the first semester based on your pre-university results), faculty-specific bursaries that are distributed at the start of each academic year, corporate scholarships that accept first-year or second-year applications (several bank and GLC programmes do), and need-based financial assistance through university financial aid offices. The most underused option for new students is going directly to the faculty office or Dean's office on enrollment day and asking what discretionary awards exist — many universities have small scholarship pools that are not publicly advertised and are filled on a first-come basis. MyScholar covers scholarship types at all levels including university-level awards.
