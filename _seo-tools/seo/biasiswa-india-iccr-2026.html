ICCR Scholarship for Malaysian Students 2026 — Study in India

Biasiswa ICCR India untuk Pelajar Malaysia 2026

The Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) administers a scholarship programme for students from countries with which India maintains cultural relations — including Malaysia. The ICCR scholarship funds both undergraduate and postgraduate study at Indian universities across a wide range of fields including engineering, medicine, arts, social sciences, and management. For Malaysian students, the ICCR scholarship is one of the less-competitive international scholarship routes — most Malaysian attention goes to UK, Australian, Japanese, or Korean scholarship programmes. India has several institutions ranked among the world's best in specific fields, including the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) for engineering, Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) for business, and AIIMS for medicine. The ICCR scholarship covers tuition, living allowance, and accommodation at Indian universities. Applications are typically processed through the Indian High Commission in Kuala Lumpur. The Malaysian community in India is small but established. MyScholar lists the ICCR scholarship for students exploring international study beyond the traditional Malaysian destinations.
