Internship Kuching Sarawak 2026

Kuching is Sarawak's capital and the administrative and commercial centre of Malaysia's largest state. Its internship market reflects Sarawak's resource-rich economy: Petronas and its contractors operate extensively in Sarawak's offshore and onshore oil and gas fields, and internships in operations, engineering, and HSE roles are available through this pipeline. The Sarawak government — through SEDC, Perbadanan Pembangunan Perumahan, and state agencies — also runs public sector internship placements, particularly for public administration and social sciences students. Banking internships are available through Affin Bank, Maybank, CIMB, and BSN branches in Kuching, following the same pattern as peninsular internship banking placements. For UNIMAS and UITM Sarawak students especially, Kuching internships allow full industrial training completion without the cost of relocating to the peninsula.
