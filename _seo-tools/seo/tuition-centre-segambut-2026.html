Pusat Tuisyen Segambut Kuala Lumpur 2026

Segambut sits in the northwest of Kuala Lumpur, between Kepong and the city centre, and is home to a mixed residential community with a notable Chinese-medium school presence. This shapes local tuition demand: parents here often look for centres that can support both national school and SRJK (C) students, sometimes for the same subject.
