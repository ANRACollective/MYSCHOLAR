Internship Duration Requirements for Malaysian Universities 2026

Tempoh Internship Universiti Malaysia 2026

Malaysian university internship duration requirements vary by institution and faculty. Most public universities require a minimum of 12 to 24 weeks of industrial training. UiTM typically requires two semesters of latihan industri for degree students. UTM and UPM engineering faculties usually require a minimum of 16 weeks. Business and accounting faculties at most universities run 12-week programs. Private universities like Taylor's, Sunway, and HELP often have shorter six to twelve week requirements. Some faculties require you to submit your company acceptance letter to the faculty office before starting, and only internships at registered companies will be approved. Always check your specific faculty handbook before accepting a position, and confirm the company is willing to provide a supporting letter and supervisor sign-off.
