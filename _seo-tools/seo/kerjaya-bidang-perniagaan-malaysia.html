Business Careers in Malaysia 2026 — All Pathways Compared

Kerjaya Perniagaan Malaysia 2026 — Semua Laluan Dibandingkan

Business is the most popular undergraduate field of study in Malaysia and also the most varied in terms of career outcomes. A business degree can lead to accounting and audit, financial analysis and investment, marketing and brand management, human resources, operations and supply chain, entrepreneurship, or corporate finance — and the outcomes depend heavily on which specialisation you pursue, which professional qualifications you add, and where you intern during your degree. Malaysian students in the business field often pursue professional qualifications alongside or after their degree — ACCA, CIMA, or CPA Australia for accountants; CFA for finance professionals; CIPP or CIPD for HR specialists. Big 4 accounting firms (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG) are major graduate employers. Banks (Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank, RHB) recruit heavily from business faculties. The corporate sector, GLCs (Khazanah, Permodalan Nasional, Sime Darby), and government-linked financial institutions also absorb large numbers of business graduates. MyJourney has dedicated paths for Accountant, Marketing Executive, and Financial Analyst.

Perniagaan adalah bidang pengajian ijazah pertama yang paling popular di Malaysia dan juga paling pelbagai dari segi hasil kerjaya. Ijazah perniagaan boleh membawa kepada perakaunan dan audit, analisis kewangan dan pelaburan, pemasaran dan pengurusan jenama, sumber manusia, operasi dan rantaian bekalan, atau kewangan korporat.
