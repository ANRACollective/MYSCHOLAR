Internship Pengurusan Acara Malaysia 2026

Event management internships in Malaysia are sought after by students from hospitality, business, and communications programmes and are concentrated around Kuala Lumpur, which hosts the bulk of Malaysia's corporate events, trade shows, and conventions. KLCC Convention Centre, Putrajaya International Convention Centre, and Axiata Arena all have support functions that draw intern talent. Independent event agencies in KL handle everything from product launches to government galas and regularly bring in interns to assist on live events, vendor coordination, on-site logistics, and post-event reporting. The work is irregular, deadline-driven, and sometimes physically demanding, but the exposure is broad and fast. Students should expect evening and weekend work during active event periods.
