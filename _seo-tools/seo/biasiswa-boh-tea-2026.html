Biasiswa BOH Tea Malaysia 2026 — BOH Foundation

The BOH Foundation Scholarship is funded by Boh Plantations, the company behind BOH Tea — Malaysia's most iconic tea brand, which has been cultivating tea in the Cameron Highlands since 1929. The scholarship is one of Malaysia's more distinctive corporate awards because of the heritage and visibility of the BOH brand, and it is often included on application shortlists by students looking for lower-competition alternatives to the major bank and GLC scholarships. The BOH Foundation scholarship is primarily awarded to students from plantation worker or agricultural community backgrounds, in keeping with BOH's roots as an agricultural company with long-standing ties to its estate workers and their families. Academic results, financial need, and community contribution are the main evaluation criteria. Applications typically open once a year. MyScholar lists the BOH Foundation scholarship alongside other foundation awards so you can compare coverage and eligibility.
