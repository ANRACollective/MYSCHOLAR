Scholarships for Creative Arts and Design Students in Malaysia 2026

Biasiswa Seni Kreatif & Design Malaysia 2026

Scholarships for creative fields in Malaysia are fewer but they exist, and the selection criteria are genuinely different from STEM scholarships. For architecture students, Gamuda and IJM have historically supported built-environment students. For graphic design and communication visual, Astro's scholarship arm and media companies occasionally fund creative candidates. Sunway University and Taylor's run their own internal creative scholarships with portfolio requirements — these are more accessible than government options. For fine arts and performing arts, Yayasan Seni and the Culture Ministry's education arm (Akademi Seni Budaya dan Warisan Kebangsaan, ASWARA) have specific grants for students entering the national arts pipeline. If you are applying for a creative scholarship, your portfolio is your essay. A mediocre CGPA with a strong portfolio beats a 4.0 with no work to show. MyScholar lists creative field scholarships separately so you can find them without scrolling through engineering and medical options.
