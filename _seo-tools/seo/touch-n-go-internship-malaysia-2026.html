Internship Touch n Go Malaysia 2026

Touch n Go Digital, the company behind Malaysia's most widely used e-wallet, is one of the more interesting internship destinations for students interested in fintech, product, and data. TNG Digital is a joint venture between Axiata's fintech arm and Ant Group, based in Bangsar South, Kuala Lumpur. With over 19 million active users, the scale of problems interns are exposed to is larger than at most Malaysian companies. Internship roles at TNG typically span product management, data analytics, engineering, marketing, and operations. Applications go through LinkedIn and the company's career portal.
