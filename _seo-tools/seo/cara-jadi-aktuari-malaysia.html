How to Become an Actuary in Malaysia — Full Career Path

Cara Jadi Aktuari di Malaysia — Laluan Kerjaya Lengkap

Actuary consistently ranks among the highest-paid graduate professions in Malaysia — and one of the most mathematically demanding. Actuaries model financial risk for insurance companies, takaful operators, pension funds, and consulting firms. The career path starts with a Bachelor of Actuarial Science degree; the main universities in Malaysia offering this programme are UM, UPM, UiTM, and a few private institutions including Heriot-Watt Malaysia, which has a particularly strong actuarial programme given its UK parent institution's reputation. After your degree, the real work begins: professional actuarial exams. Most Malaysian actuaries pursue either the Society of Actuaries (SOA) route from the US or the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA) route from the UK. These exams are notoriously difficult — candidates typically pass one or two exams per year while working full-time, and reaching Fellowship status takes seven to ten years. The Malaysian Insurance Institute (MII) also offers a local fellowship pathway. Major employers include AIA Malaysia, Great Eastern, Prudential, Etiqa Takaful, MNRB Re, and the large actuarial consulting firms (Mercer, Willis Towers Watson, Milliman) operating out of KL. Starting salary for a fresh actuarial graduate in Malaysia is RM 3,500–5,000, rising sharply with each exam passed — fully qualified Fellows typically earn RM 15,000–30,000 or more.

Aktuari adalah salah satu profesion bergaji tertinggi di Malaysia. Mereka mengira risiko kewangan untuk syarikat insurans dan takaful. Laluan bermula dengan ijazah Sains Aktuari, diikuti peperiksaan profesional SOA atau IFoA yang mengambil masa bertahun-tahun untuk diselesaikan.
