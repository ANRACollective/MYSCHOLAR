Career Paths for Malaysian Students — See the Full Route Before You Commit

Laluan Kerjaya untuk Pelajar Malaysia — Lihat Laluan Penuh Sebelum Komit

Most career advice for Malaysian students comes from two places: parents who graduated in a different economy, and generic websites that describe jobs without explaining how to actually get into them in Malaysia. MyJourney is built to fix that gap. It maps 20+ career pathways specific to the Malaysian context — covering the SPM subjects you need, which pre-university route makes sense, which Malaysian universities offer relevant degrees, what the regulatory body registration process looks like, and what you can realistically expect to earn at different stages. Every career on MyJourney also links directly to relevant scholarships on MyScholar (so you can see which scholarships fund that path) and to relevant internships on MyIntern (so you can see where students actually get early experience in that field). It is still early days and we are adding more careers regularly, but the 20+ currently live are among the most-searched by Malaysian students. If you already have a career in mind, go directly to that career page. If you are still figuring it out, the category pages for health, engineering, technology, business, and law are good places to start.
