How to Prepare for an Internship Interview in Malaysia 2026

Cara Bersedia untuk Temuduga Internship Malaysia 2026

The difference between a prepared and unprepared candidate is visible within the first two minutes of a Malaysian internship interview. Before you go in, spend thirty minutes on the company's website and LinkedIn — know their main product or service, a recent news item, and which division you are joining. Bring two printed copies of your resume even if you submitted it online. Arrive ten minutes early. If it is a virtual interview, test your audio and camera the night before. During the interview, pause before answering, answer with a real example when you can, and ask at least one genuine question at the end. After, send a short thank-you email within 24 hours. Most candidates do not, and it is noticed. MyScholar lists active internship openings to give you a real company to prepare for.

Perbezaan antara calon yang bersedia dan tidak bersedia terlihat dalam dua minit pertama temuduga internship Malaysia. Sebelum masuk, luangkan tiga puluh minit di laman web dan LinkedIn syarikat — tahu produk atau perkhidmatan utama mereka, satu berita terkini, dan bahagian yang anda akan sertai. Bawa dua salinan bercetak resume walaupun anda hantar secara dalam talian. Tiba sepuluh minit awal. Jika temuduga dalam talian, uji audio dan kamera malam sebelumnya. Semasa temuduga, berhenti sebentar sebelum menjawab, jawab dengan contoh sebenar apabila boleh, dan tanya sekurang-kurangnya satu soalan yang ikhlas di akhir. Selepas itu, hantar emel terima kasih ringkas dalam masa 24 jam.
