How to Apply for a MARA Scholarship in Malaysia 2026

Cara Mohon Biasiswa MARA 2026

MARA offers two main scholarship programmes: the Tertiary Education Sponsorship Programme (TESP) for diploma and degree-level studies, and the Young Talent Development Programme (YTP) for high-performing Bumiputera students going to top international universities. Both are open exclusively to Bumiputera applicants. Applications for TESP are submitted through the MARA online portal. For YTP, the process is more selective and includes a separate registration window. Key eligibility points: Malaysian citizen and Bumiputera status, academic results above MARA's minimum threshold (varies by programme), and active co-curriculum involvement. For TESP local study, CGPA 3.0 minimum is standard. For YTP and overseas study tracks, results must be well above that. MARA also requires proof of family income — priority is given to B40 and lower M40 households. After academic screening, shortlisted candidates attend a panel interview conducted in Bahasa Malaysia. MARA menawarkan dua program biasiswa utama: Program Penajaan Pendidikan Tinggi (TESP) untuk pengajian peringkat diploma dan ijazah, dan Program Pembangunan Bakat Muda (YTP) untuk pelajar Bumiputera berprestasi tinggi yang pergi ke universiti antarabangsa terkemuka. Kedua-duanya terbuka secara eksklusif kepada pemohon Bumiputera. Untuk TESP, permohonan diserahkan melalui portal dalam talian MARA. Kelayakan utama: warganegara Malaysia dan status Bumiputera, keputusan akademik melebihi ambang minimum MARA, dan penglibatan aktif dalam ko-kurikulum.
