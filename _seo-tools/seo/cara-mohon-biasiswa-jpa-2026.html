How to Apply for a JPA Scholarship in Malaysia 2026

Cara Mohon Biasiswa JPA 2026

JPA (Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam) runs two main scholarship tracks: Program Khas (for SPM leavers going into pre-university) and Program Penajaan Nasional (PPN) for STPM, diploma, and first-degree applicants. Applications are submitted through the online JPA scholarship portal, typically opening around March and closing in April each year. Required documents include MyKad, SPM or STPM results slip, parents' income statement (salary slip or EPF statement), school co-curriculum records, and a personal statement. For Program Khas, only open and transparent school positions count toward co-curriculum scoring — sports, clubs, and uniformed bodies. The selection process is competitive: JPA receives tens of thousands of applications and shortlists based on academic results combined with co-curriculum points. After shortlisting, selected candidates are called for a panel interview. MyScholar lists both JPA scholarship programs with current status so you know exactly when to apply.

JPA (Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam) menjalankan dua trek biasiswa utama: Program Khas (untuk lepasan SPM yang memasuki pra-universiti) dan Program Penajaan Nasional (PPN) untuk pemohon STPM, diploma, dan ijazah pertama. Permohonan diserahkan melalui portal biasiswa JPA dalam talian, biasanya dibuka sekitar Mac dan ditutup pada April setiap tahun. Dokumen yang diperlukan termasuk MyKad, slip keputusan SPM atau STPM, penyata pendapatan ibu bapa (slip gaji atau penyata KWSP), rekod ko-kurikulum sekolah, dan penyataan peribadi. MyScholar menyenaraikan kedua-dua program biasiswa JPA dengan status semasa.
