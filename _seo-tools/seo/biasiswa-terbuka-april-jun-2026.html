Scholarships Open in April–June 2026 in Malaysia

Biasiswa Terbuka April–Jun 2026 di Malaysia

The April to June window is one of the most active scholarship application periods in Malaysia, driven by SPM results release in March and the subsequent wave of Form 5 leavers entering the scholarship market. Major scholarships that typically open in this window include JPA Program Khas, MARA TESP, Bank Negara Malaysia scholarships, and several corporate and GLC scholarships tied to the academic calendar. For current university students, this is also when many semester-break internship applications open — companies post July intakes from April onwards. The most important thing to do now: identify your shortlist, check each scholarship's requirements against your actual results, and prepare your documents before the deadlines stack up. Many students lose out not because they are underqualified but because they applied for too many scholarships superficially instead of a few well. MyScholar updates scholarship statuses as they open and close, so check back regularly during this window.
