How to Apply for a MARA Scholarship in Malaysia — Complete 2026 Guide

Cara Mohon Biasiswa MARA 2026 — Panduan Lengkap

MARA (Majlis Amanah Rakyat) is one of the largest scholarship providers in Malaysia, offering programmes exclusively for bumiputera students. Unlike JPA, MARA runs several distinct scholarship streams: the Biasiswa Kecil Persekutuan (local diploma and degree), Biasiswa Pelajaran Luar Negara (overseas degree), and various sector-specific scholarships in professional fields. Applications are made through the MARA Online application portal (biasiswa.mara.gov.my). Timelines differ by programme but many open between January and April following SPM results. MARA's requirements are generally less stringent than JPA on raw academic grades — particularly for applicants from lower-income families — but the application still requires a strong co-curricular profile, a clear field choice, and a compelling case for why you deserve the opportunity. The interview process for MARA overseas scholarships is structured but less intimidating than the JPA panel format for many applicants. One key difference from JPA: MARA scholarships often come with a bond obligation to serve in the public or private sector upon return. The bond terms vary by programme and are worth reading carefully before accepting. MyScholar lists MARA scholarships alongside eligibility, value, and current status.

Biasiswa MARA ditawarkan khusus untuk pelajar bumiputera melalui portal biasiswa.mara.gov.my. MARA menawarkan pelbagai skim — dari biasiswa diploma tempatan hingga biasiswa ijazah luar negara. Syarat akademik MARA sedikit lebih fleksibel berbanding JPA, tetapi ikatan perkhidmatan biasanya dikenakan.
