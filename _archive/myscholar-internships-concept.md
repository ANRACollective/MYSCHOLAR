# MyScholar Internships — Concept One-Pager
*Internal reference document · April 2026*

---

## The idea in one sentence

Add a free, curated internship directory inside MyScholar so Malaysian students have one place for both scholarships and internships — then grow the internship side into a collaboration hub and revenue layer over time.

---

## Why it belongs inside MyScholar

The scholarship and internship user is the same person. A Form 5 or first-year degree student looking for financial aid is the same student who needs work experience to strengthen their scholarship application. Keeping both under myscholar.my means they never have to leave, and the internship section inherits the trust and traffic MyScholar has already built.

A standalone product would start from zero. This doesn't have to.

---

## The problem it solves

There is no clean, student-focused internship directory for Malaysia. JobStreet and LinkedIn are cluttered with full-time roles. MYFutureWorks exists but is poorly maintained. Graduan and Hiredly skew toward fresh graduates. No one has done for internships what MyScholar does for scholarships.

---

## What it looks like at launch (MVP)

- A tab or section at myscholar.my/internships
- 30 to 50 curated, recurring internship programmes — structured annual intakes from companies like Khazanah, Maybank, TNB, Petronas, government agencies, and GLCs
- Same format as scholarships: company name, field, duration, eligibility, application link, deadline
- Free, no login required
- Mobile-first, same design system as the rest of MyScholar

---

## Growth arc

**Phase 1 — Free directory**
Curated by ANRA. Same operating model as scholarships. Builds student trust and company visibility. No revenue, full credibility.

**Phase 2 — Featured listings**
Companies notice students finding them through MyScholar. Introduce optional paid featured placements or verified badges. Small fee, meaningful signal for employers.

**Phase 3 — Collaboration hub**
Companies manage their own listings directly. Get analytics on student interest and application intent. MyScholar becomes the pipeline between Malaysian students and employers — not just a directory but an active channel.

---

## Business model principle

The scholarship directory stays permanently free. That is the mission and the trust signal.

The internship side evolves into the revenue layer. One funds the other. If anyone asks how MyScholar makes money: scholarships are a public good, internships are where we work with industry.

---

## What to do right now

Nothing yet. MyScholar's scholarship directory is still maturing — 277 entries, Bahasa feature in progress, first collaboration just landed. The internship idea is parked here as a roadmap item.

Revisit in Q3 2026. If students are asking for it and the scholarship side is stable, build it as a section inside myscholar.my. No new domain needed at that point.

---

## Early signals to watch for

- Students or partners asking about internships unprompted
- FSTEP or similar collaborators mentioning internship awareness as a gap
- Traffic or search queries on myscholar.my that suggest internship intent
