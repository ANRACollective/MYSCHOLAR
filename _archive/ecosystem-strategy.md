# MyScholar Ecosystem Strategy
**Last updated:** April 2026 | **Owner:** ANRA

---

## The Core Idea

MyScholar is not three products. It is one platform serving three life stages of a Malaysian student's journey.

**MyScholar (Funding)** → helps students find scholarships to fund their education  
**MyTuition (Learning)** → helps students find quality tuition to perform academically  
**MyIntern (Launch)** → helps graduates transition into the workforce

Each product solves a real, distinct problem. Together they form a student lifecycle operating system — the only platform a Malaysian student needs from Form 4 to first job.

---

## The Three User Groups

**Students** are the daily active layer. They use all three products at different stages. They need speed, clarity, and relevance — not noise. Every feature decision should first ask: does this help a student find what they need faster?

**Parents** are the trust and conversion layer. They influence which tuition centres are chosen, whether scholarships are worth applying to, and how the platform is shared. Parents share by word of mouth. Earning parent trust is how MyScholar scales without paid acquisition.

**Graduates** are the outcome layer. A student who found a scholarship on MyScholar, improved via tuition, and got their first internship through MyIntern is a walking case study. These users become advocates and eventually mentors.

---

## The Natural User Journey

A student discovers MyScholar looking for a scholarship in Form 5.  
They find one. They return during university applications.  
A parent sees the site. They look for tuition for a younger sibling.  
The original student graduates. They check MyIntern.  
They land their first role. They share the platform with coursemates.

This loop runs without paid advertising. It only requires that each product is genuinely useful at its moment.

## Product Priorities by Stage

### Now (Apr–Jun 2026): Seed and stabilise
- MyScholar: maintain data quality, add deadline alerts, keep SEO healthy
- MyTuition: get KELLY running, seed KL database, soft launch
- MyIntern: add stipend benchmarks, improve company profiles

### Mid-term (Jul–Dec 2026): Deepen each product
- MyScholar: success stories, application guides by scholarship type, email cadence
- MyTuition: verified listings badge, parent reviews, results data, Penang/JB expansion
- MyIntern: graduate program tracking, company culture snapshots, industry guides

### Long-term (2027+): Connect the ecosystem
- Cross-product user journey (student bookmarks scholarships + tuition + internships in one profile)
- MyScholar for Parents landing experience
- Potential monetisation: verified listings, featured placements, employer partnerships

## Brand Positioning

MyScholar is not "a scholarship website" or "a tuition directory."

MyScholar is **Malaysia's student success ecosystem** — the platform that helps Malaysian students fund their education, perform academically, and launch their careers.

This positioning is earned, not declared. You earn it by having three working products with real data. Until then, it lives in this doc and informs every product decision.

---

## Homepage Direction (Future State)

When all three products are stable, the homepage should evolve to reflect the ecosystem:

**Section 1 — Who are you?**  
Student / Parent / Graduate  
Each routes to a tailored experience.

**Section 2 — What do you need right now?**  
Dynamic cards based on persona:  
- Pay for studies → Scholarships  
- Improve your grades → Tuition  
- Start your career → Internships

**Section 3 — The lifecycle story**  
A simple visual: Student → Learner → Intern → Professional  
Anchored in Malaysian student reality, not aspirational copy.

**Not yet. Build the products first. Redesign the homepage when you have three things worth pointing at.**

---

## What to Avoid

- Rebranding any product before the data is solid
- Launching a homepage redesign before all three products are live
- Adding features that don't pass the student-utility test
- Over-engineering the ecosystem narrative before it's earned

---

## Design Languages (Fixed — Do Not Mix)

| Product | Aesthetic | Background | Accent |
|---|---|---|---|
| MyScholar | Light / ice | White + cool greys | Blue |
| MyIntern | Dark glass | Near-black | Teal/dark |
| MyTuition | Warm linen | `#f8f4ee` cream | Terracotta `#b85c3a` |

These are intentionally distinct. Each product has its own visual identity. The ecosystem is expressed through navigation and cross-links, not by making them look the same.
