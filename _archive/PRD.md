# PRD — MyScholar.my
*Last updated: April 2026 | Status: Living document*

---

## Problem

Scholarship discovery in Malaysia is fragmented. Official portals are hard to navigate, deadlines are buried in PDFs, and students — especially B40 and rural — miss out not because they're ineligible, but because they never heard of the programme.

Same problem for internships. JobStreet mixes them with full-time jobs. MYFutureJobs is poorly maintained. No clean, student-focused internship directory exists for Malaysia.

---

## Solution

One free site. Two directories. Everything a Malaysian student needs to find scholarships and internships — searchable, mobile-first, no login, no friction.

**myscholar.my** — scholarships  
**myscholar.my/internships** — internships

---

## Users

**Primary:** SPM/STPM/A-level leavers and undergrads. Mobile-first. High urgency. Time-sensitive.  
**Secondary:** Parents, school counsellors, postgrad students.

**Persona test:** Every feature decision is measured against Aleya — 18, B40 Kelantan, SPM results just out, on her phone, needs to know every scholarship she qualifies for in under 10 minutes. If it slows her down, it doesn't ship.

---

## Core Features

### Scholarships (index.html)
- Search by name, provider, field, keywords
- Filter by: category, entry level, destination, type, deadline urgency
- Card view → detail modal (amount, eligibility, deadline, tips, official link)
- Bookmark (localStorage, no login)
- Correction submission form
- URL health status indicator

### Internships (internships.html)
- Same interaction model, dark glass design
- Filter by: industry, study level, rolling intake, allowance, Bumiputera
- Company logo via Clearbit domain lookup
- Allowance display (with `(estimated)` flag if from forum research)
- Detail modal: roles, duration, intake schedule, eligibility, tips, verified date

### SEO Layer
- 70+ static landing pages targeting Malay + English scholarship/internship queries
- Auto-generated by Jack agent from keyword manifest
- Served from GitHub Pages, indexed via sitemap

---

## Non-Goals (MVP)

- User accounts or authentication
- Application tracking
- Email alerts
- Company dashboards or paid placements
- Native app
- Bahasa Malaysia full-site toggle *(in progress, not MVP)*

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Signal |
|--------|--------|
| Monthly active users | Growing MoM |
| Search engagement | Majority of sessions include a `search_query` event |
| Mobile traffic | Maintain 80%+ |
| Scholarship URL health | < 5% in `suspected` or `broken` status |
| Internship record count | 50+ visible records |
| Correction rate | Low = data is accurate |
| Inbound partner interest | Companies or providers reaching out via hello@myscholar.my |

## Constraints

- Everything stays in single HTML files. No separate CSS/JS. No build step.
- Scholarship count in UI copy is permanently **"200+"** — do not change.
- Scholarships are permanently free. Revenue comes only from the internship side.
- Mobile-first: test every UI change at 390px first.
- index.html = light/ice aesthetic. internships.html = dark glass. Don't mix.
